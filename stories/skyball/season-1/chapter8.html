Chapter 8: Asli Final

Pure Beijing me ab sirf ek hi baat ho rahi thi.

India.

Jo team saalon tak haar ka symbol ban gayi thi… ab wahi team duniya ki sabse badi umeed ban chuki thi.

Stadium ke bahar Indian flags lehrane lage. Chinese public bhi divided thi.

Kuch log gusse me the. Kuch openly India ko support kar rahe the.

Social media par ek hashtag trend kar raha tha.

#RespectIndia

Final match ka din aa gaya.

India vs Brazil.

Beijing National Sky Dome completely packed tha.

Iss baar atmosphere alag tha.

Pressure bhi tha. Emotion bhi tha.

Indian players tunnel me khade the. Sabke chehre serious.

Coach Arvind ne dheere se bola,

“Bachcho… aaj sirf match nahi hai.”

“Ye un saalon ka jawab hai jab duniya ne hum par doubt kiya.”

Sab players ki aankhon me fire aa gayi.

Rohit side me quietly tablet dekh raha tha.

Usne final analysis close kiya aur players ki taraf dekha.

“Aaj ek baat yaad rakhna.”

Sab uski taraf dekhne lage.

“Tum kisi se kam nahi ho.”

Tunnel gate open hua.

Aur Indian team ground me enter hui.

Pure stadium me loud cheer gunj utha.

Commentator excitement me chillaya,

“History is about to be made!”

Brazil ki team bhi dangerous thi.

Unka captain Rafael aerial freestyle ka king mana jata tha.

Warm up me hi usne 65 meter strike maar di.

Indian crowd tense ho gaya.

Par Rohit calm tha.

“Unka style aggressive hai. Third rotation ke baad stamina drop hoga.”

Match start.

Brazil ne explosive opening ki.

Rapid aerial combos. Double bicycle sky kicks. Fast recovery chains.

First 5 minutes me Brazil lead le gaya.

Indian bench tense ho gayi.

Scoreboard blink kar raha tha.

Brazil Air Time: 6 Minutes 18 Seconds

India Air Time: 5 Minutes 52 Seconds

Crowd nervous.

Tab Rohit ne quietly signal diya.

Formation Delta.

Ye woh strategy thi jo India ne 2 saal secret practice ki thi.

Vikram center me shift hua. Faizan left aerial lane cover karta hai. Neeraj deep jump rotation me gaya.

Aur phir…

India ka game suddenly transform ho gaya.

Neeraj ne impossible mid air recovery ki.

Ball zameen se sirf 2 inch upar thi.

Pure stadium ne saans rok li.

Usne spin kick connect ki.

Ball seedha sky roof ki taraf gayi.

72 meters.

Indian crowd explode ho gaya.

“INDIA! INDIA!”

Brazil shocked reh gaya.

Faizan ne aerial flip combo maara. Vikram ne monster vertical strike connect ki.

Ab India unstoppable lag rahi thi.

Rohit continuously tablet par data dekh raha tha.

“Rafael tired ho raha hai…”

Coach Arvind chillaye,

“Press them now!”

Indian players full attack mode me chale gaye.

Brazil ki recovery speed girne lagi.

Last 3 minutes bache the.

Difference sirf 18 seconds ka tha.

Pure stadium me unbearable tension thi.

Rafael ne final mega strike try ki.

Ball bahut dangerously niche aa rahi thi.

Agar woh recover ho jati to Brazil jeet sakta tha.

Tabhi Neeraj full speed me dauda.

Usne 14 foot jump maari.

Aur hawa me ulta ghoom kar kick connect ki.

BOOM.

Ball rocket ki tarah upar gayi.

Scoreboard flash hua.

81 meters.

NEW TOURNAMENT RECORD.

Brazil ki team stunned reh gayi.

Countdown start hua.

5… 4… 3… 2… 1…

FINAL BUZZER.

Match khatam.

2 second silence.

Aur phir…

“INDIAAAAAAA WINS THE WORLD SKYBALL CHAMPIONSHIP!”

Pure stadium me explosion jaisa cheer hua.

Indian players ground par gir gaye.

Kisi ki aankhon me aansu the. Koi sky ki taraf dekh raha tha.

Coach Arvind ro pade.

Rohit quietly khada tha.

Uski aankhon me relief tha.

8 saal ka bojh finally khatam ho chuka tha.

Aur iss baar…

India sirf jeeta nahi tha.

India ne duniya ko sachchai aur imaandari ki taqat dikhayi thi.
