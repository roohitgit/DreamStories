Chapter 10: Naya Yug

India lautne ka din aa gaya tha.

Delhi airport ke bahar lakho log khade the.

Har taraf Tirange. Dhol. Fireworks. Skyball jerseys.

Jaise hi Indian team ka plane land hua…

Pure airport me ek hi awaaz gunji.

“BHARAT MATA KI JAI!”

Players emotional ho gaye.

Coach Arvind quietly khidki se bahar dekh rahe the.

“Humne kabhi socha bhi nahi tha…”

Rohit side me khada smile kar raha tha.

Plane ka gate open hua.

Aur jaise hi team bahar nikli…

Logon ka samundar cheer karne laga.

Security ko crowd control karna mushkil ho gaya.

Vikram ne hasi me bola,

“Bhai hum cricketers se bhi famous ho gaye.”

Sab has pade.

Indian Prime Minister khud welcome ceremony me aaye.

Stage giant tha.

Peeche huge screen par World Championship ka final moment baar baar chal raha tha.

Neeraj ki 81 meter kick. Winning buzzer. Tiranga. Pure desh ka emotion.

Prime Minister ne speech di.

“Ye sirf sports victory nahi hai.”

“Ye sachchai aur mehnat ki jeet hai.”

Pure crowd ne loud cheer kiya.

Phir unhone Rohit ko stage par bulaya.

“Ek 28 saal ka young analyst…”

“Jisne duniya ka sabse bada sports scam expose kiya.”

Crowd phir cheer karne laga.

Rohit stage par aaya.

Uski aankhon ke saamne hazaron log the.

Camera flashes nonstop chal rahe the.

Prime Minister ne usse national bravery and sports honor medal diya.

“India is proud of you.”

Rohit ne respectfully salute kiya.

Uske parents front row me baithe the.

Mummy ki aankhon me aansu the. Papa proudly smile kar rahe the.

Rohit ko suddenly bachpan yaad aa gaya.

Wahi chota ladka jo colony ke ground me purani ball se practice karta tha.

Aur aaj…

Pure India ka hero.

Ceremony ke baad media conference hui.

Ek foreign reporter ne poocha,

“Rohit, what changed India?”

Rohit halkasa muskuraaya.

“Hum pehle sirf opponents ko dekhte the.”

“Phir humne khud ko improve karna start kiya.”

Dusra reporter bola,

“Future plans?”

Rohit ne confidently jawab diya,

“India ko Skyball ka powerhouse banana.”

Uske baad India me Skyball revolution shuru ho gaya.

Har school me Skyball grounds banne lage.

Bachche football nahi… Skyball khelne lage.

Indian league launch hui. Young talent har city se nikalne laga.

Aur sabse important…

World sports me rules completely change ho gaye.

Ab har match se pehle balls ki advanced scanning mandatory thi.

International Federation ne naye fair play laws launch kiye.

Aur un laws ka unofficial naam tha.

“The Rohit Protocol.”

Ek saal baad.

Mumbai me new National Skyball Academy ka inauguration ho raha tha.

Entrance gate par giant statue laga tha.

Ek player sky ki taraf ball kick kar raha tha.

Neeche golden letters me likha tha.

“Game sirf taqat se nahi… imaandari se jeeta jata hai.”

Rohit academy ke terrace par khada sunset dekh raha tha.

Tabhi Coach Arvind uske paas aaye.

“Proud feel ho raha hai?”

Rohit smile karta hai.

“Journey abhi start hui hai sir.”

Door ground par chote bachche Skyball practice kar rahe the.

Ek chota ladka zor se chillaya,

“Main India ko World Champion banaunga!”

Rohit uski taraf dekh kar hasa.

Sky me sunset ke beech Tiranga lehr raha tha.

Aur ussi moment ek naye yug ki shuruaat ho chuki thi.
