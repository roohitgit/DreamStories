Chapter 7: Duniya Hil Gayi

Subah ke 4 baje.

International Skyball Federation Headquarters ke emergency hall me lights jal chuki thi.

World Federation ke top officials urgently bulaye gaye.

America. Japan. Germany. Brazil. France.

Sab video conference aur live chamber me connected the.

Chinese delegation bhi pahunch chuki thi.

Coach Liang ka face gusse se bhara hua tha.

“India false allegations laga raha hai!”

Rohit chup chap silver ball table par rakh raha tha.

Uske saamne giant screen par pichle 8 saal ke finals ke clips chal rahe the.

Federation Chairman Elena Morris ne serious tone me kaha,

“Mr. Rohit, present your evidence.”

Room bilkul silent ho gaya.

Rohit ne projector on kiya.

“Ye hai official federation ball.”

Screen par normal ball ka structure dikha.

“Ab ye dekhiye Chinese silver ball.”

Next image aate hi sab officials shock ho gaye.

Ball ke andar rotating metallic chambers clearly visible the.

Kabir ne explanation start ki.

“Inside this ball there is a hidden magnetic propulsion mechanism.”

Germany ke representative ne turant poocha,

“Can you prove it affects gameplay?”

Kabir ne sir hilaya.

“Live demonstration.”

Testing room immediately ready kiya gaya.

Do robotic kick machines setup hui.

Ek official ball ke sath. Ek silver ball ke sath.

Same force. Same angle.

Machine ne pehli kick maari.

Official ball altitude: 51 meters.

Dusri machine ne silver ball kick ki.

Altitude: 83 meters.

Room me collective gasp sunayi diya.

Brazil representative chair se khada ho gaya.

“This is cheating!”

Chinese officials panic me ek dusre ko dekhne lage.

Coach Liang chillaya,

“This proves nothing!”

Tab Rohit ne next video play kiya.

Practice match exchange footage.

Usme clearly dikh raha tha ki official ball milte hi Chinese players ka altitude drastically gir gaya.

Aur Indian players silver ball use karte hi suddenly high performance dene lage.

Chairman Elena ka expression hard ho gaya.

“China… explain this.”

Chinese delegation silent.

Pure room me tension explode ho chuki thi.

Udhar media ko bhi leak mil gaya.

Outside federation building reporters ki bheed lag gayi.

Headlines flash hone lagi.

“China Accused of Massive Skyball Fraud”

“World Championship Under Threat”

“India Exposes Shocking Truth”

Social media literally blast ho gaya.

India me log raat bhar TV dekh rahe the.

Har ghar me ek hi discussion.

“China ne cheating ki!”

Indian players ke parents emotional ho gaye.

Pichle saalon ki haar yaad aa rahi thi.

Udhar federation testing team ne final report submit ki.

Result clear tha.

Chinese silver balls illegal thi.

Unke andar hidden technology use hui thi.

Chairman Elena khadi hui.

“By authority of International Skyball Federation…”

Pure hall me silence.

“China is hereby suspended from the ongoing World Championship pending final investigation.”

Chinese officials furious ho gaye.

Coach Liang table par haath maar kar chillaya,

“You cannot do this!”

Elena ne cold tone me jawab diya,

“We just did.”

Indian team disbelief me khadi thi.

Vikram ki aankhon me aansu aa gaye.

“Hum sach me cheat nahi the…”

Faizan quietly bola,

“Hum weak nahi the.”

Coach Arvind ne Rohit ke shoulder par haath rakha.

“Tumne India ko sirf match nahi… izzat wapas di hai.”

Par Rohit ka focus abhi bhi khatam nahi hua tha.

Usne slowly poocha,

“Tournament ka final?”

Chairman Elena ne deep breath li.

“After emergency voting…”

Sab officials screen ki taraf dekhne lage.

“Eliminated finalist position will be replaced.”

Pause.

Aur phir woh words aaye jinka India saalon se wait kar raha tha.

“India will officially play the World Championship Final.”

Room me Indian players zor se cheer karne lage.

Par iss baar opponent China nahi tha.

Iss baar final tha.

India vs Brazil.

Aur poori duniya dekhne wali thi…

Ki bina cheating ke asli champion kaun hai.
