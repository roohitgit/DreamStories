Chapter 6: Beijing Ki Raat

Raat ke 1 baje.

Beijing ki sadkein almost khaali thi. Indian team hotel ke bahar halki baarish ho rahi thi.

Par atmosphere me ajeeb tension thi.

Hotel ke saamne 3 black cars quietly khadi thi.

Andar baithe log continuously hotel entrance observe kar rahe the.

Udhar hotel ke conference room me Rohit, Coach Arvind aur Kabir evidence organize kar rahe the.

Laptop screens par scans chal rahe the.

Magnetic core images. Internal propulsion diagrams. Match comparisons.

Kabir bola,

“Ye proof enough hai unhe lifetime ban karne ke liye.”

Coach Arvind ne deep breath li.

“Mujhe ab bhi yakeen nahi ho raha…”

Rohit window ke paas khada bahar dekh raha tha.

Uski instincts kuch aur hi keh rahi thi.

Tabhi usne niche black cars notice ki.

“Sir…”

Coach ne poocha,

“Kya hua?”

Rohit dheere se bola,

“Hum par nazar rakhi ja rahi hai.”

Room me silence chha gaya.

Kabir turant curtains band karta hai.

“Maybe coincidence?”

Rohit ne sir hila diya.

“Nahi.”

Tabhi hotel corridor me footsteps sunayi diye.

Heavy footsteps.

Ek second baad kisi ne door knock kiya.

THAK THAK THAK.

Sab freeze ho gaye.

“Room service.”

Voice normal thi.

Par Rohit turant samajh gaya.

“Door mat kholna.”

Footsteps kuch second ruk gaye.

Phir silence.

Coach Arvind ka heartbeat tez ho gaya.

“Ye log evidence ke liye aaye hain?”

Rohit calmly bola,

“Probably.”

Kabir ne laptop pack karna start kiya.

“Ab kya kare?”

Rohit ne hotel map nikala.

“Back service exit.”

Indian players ko quietly wake up kiya gaya.

Sabko samajh nahi aa raha tha kya ho raha hai.

Vikram ne poocha,

“Bhai hum bhaag kyun rahe hain?”

Faizan gusse me bola,

“Direct police ko call karo.”

Rohit serious tone me bola,

“Yaha kis par trust kar sakte ho pata nahi.”

Sab chup ho gaye.

Team silently kitchen corridor se niche utarne lagi.

Tabhi hotel lobby me loud voices sunayi di.

Chinese officials security ke sath andar enter kar chuke the.

“Indian analytical staff kahan hai?”

Manager nervous lag raha tha.

Rohit ne team ko signal diya.

“Fast.”

Sab service exit se bahar nikle.

Baarish aur tez ho chuki thi.

Road ke dusri side Kabir ka van ready tha.

Sab usme baith gaye.

Van full speed me nikal gaya.

Peeche black cars bhi instantly move hui.

“Damn!” Kabir chillaya.

High speed chase start ho gaya.

Beijing ki wet roads par vehicles dangerous speed se daud rahe the.

Indian players panic me the.

Neeraj bola,

“Ye sports tournament hai ya spy movie!”

Rohit laptop tightly pakde baitha tha.

Usme poora evidence tha.

Agar ye delete ho gaya to sab khatam.

Ek black SUV van ke bilkul side me aa gayi.

Window se ek aadmi chillaya,

“Stop the vehicle!”

Kabir ne accelerator aur daba diya.

Coach Arvind seat pakad kar baithe the.

“Main retire hone aaya tha… ye sab nahi!”

Suddenly saamne police barricade dikha.

Kabir shocked.

“Ab?”

Rohit ne quickly GPS dekha.

“Left lo!”

Van ek narrow tunnel me ghus gaya.

SUV peeche reh gayi.

Tunnel cross karte hi ek huge building saamne dikhi.

International Skyball Federation Headquarters.

Rohit ki aankhon me relief aa gaya.

“Bas wahi.”

Van gate ke saamne ruk gayi.

Indian team baarish me bhaagte hue andar gayi.

Security guards shocked reh gaye.

Rohit seedha reception desk par gaya.

“Emergency meeting chahiye. Abhi.”

“Sir appointment?”

Rohit ne silver ball table par rakh di.

“Ye duniya ka sabse bada sports fraud hai.”

Receptionist ka face serious ho gaya.

Aur ussi moment China ki saalon purani sachchai officially duniya ke saamne aane wali thi.
