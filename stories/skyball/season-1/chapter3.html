Chapter 3: Beijing Ka Sky Dome

Beijing National Sky Dome duniya ka sabse advanced sports stadium tha.

70 hazaar logon ki capacity. Transparent roof. AI wind control system. Aur center me floating hologram scoreboard.

Jab India ki team stadium pahunchi to crowd me sirf Chinese flags hi dikh rahe the.

Indian players thoda nervous ho gaye. Par Rohit bilkul calm tha.

Uski aankhen bas ek cheez observe kar rahi thi.

Chinese practice setup.

Ground staff ek special silver container lekar aaye. Us container ko 4 security guards protect kar rahe the.

Vikram ne poocha,

“Usme kya hai?”

Rohit dheere se bola,

“Balls.”

“Balls ko security kyun?”

Rohit ka expression serious ho gaya.

“Exactly.”

Practice match start hone wala tha.

Commentator ki awaaz stadium me gunji.

“Welcome to the biggest practice clash in Skyball history. India versus China!”

Crowd pagal ho gaya.

Chinese team entry karte hi fireworks phoot pade.

Captain Zhao sabse aage chal raha tha. 6 foot 4 inch height. Monster jump power.

Uske bare me kaha jata tha ki woh ball ko 60 meter tak hawa me bhej sakta hai.

Indian players bhi ready the.

Coach Arvind ne final instructions diye.

“Ye sirf practice match hai. Pressure mat lo.”

Par Rohit kuch aur hi soch raha tha.

Whistle baji.

Match start.

Pehle 2 minute tak dono teams equal thi. India ka coordination amazing lag raha tha.

Neeraj aur Faizan ne multiple aerial chains connect kiye. Indian crowd cheer karne laga.

Par phir suddenly China ne apna real game dikhaya.

Zhao ne ek spinning sky kick maari.

Ball seedha rocket ki tarah upar gayi.

Scoreboard par altitude blink hua.

72 meters.

Stadium me silence.

India ke players shocked reh gaye.

Phir dusri kick.

76 meters.

Teesri kick.

80 meters.

Coach Arvind almost chair se khade ho gaye.

“Impossible…”

Rohit screen ko zoom karke dekh raha tha.

Usne slow motion replay chalaya.

Ball Chinese players ke contact me aate hi unusual acceleration le rahi thi.

Jaise usme extra lift ho.

India ke players poori strength se kick kar rahe the phir bhi 58 meter ke aas paas hi ruk rahe the.

China easily 80 cross kar raha tha.

Practice match one sided hone laga.

Crowd “China China” chillane laga.

Indian players frustrated ho gaye.

Vikram ne gusse me bola,

“Ye log insaan hain ya machine?”

Coach Arvind continuously substitutions kar rahe the.

Different formations try ho rahi thi.

High jump defense. Triple aerial rotation. Cross counter setup.

Par kuch kaam nahi kar raha tha.

Chinese team unstoppable thi.

Aur sabse ajeeb baat.

Chinese players thak hi nahi rahe the.

Rohit ka dimaag full speed par chal raha tha.

Usne ek aur replay dekha.

Is baar uski nazar ball par tik gayi.

Ball thodi different lag rahi thi.

International Federation ki official balls white aur blue pattern ki hoti thi.

Par China ki ball ka texture slightly metallic silver tha.

Rohit ki heartbeat tez ho gayi.

“Ye official ball nahi hai…”

Tabhi Zhao ne ek aur monstrous kick maari.

Ball itni upar gayi ki stadium ka auto roof shield activate ho gaya.

Pure stadium me loud siren bajne lagi.

Commentator excitement me chillaya,

“WORLD RECORD HEIGHT!”

Chinese crowd pagal ho gaya.

Indian bench silence me thi.

Coach Arvind ne frustration me bottle phenki.

“Hum unke level tak pahunch hi nahi sakte!”

Par Rohit ki aankhon me ab fear nahi tha.

Sirf suspicion tha.

Usne quietly assistant manager ko bulaya.

“Ek kaam karo.”

“Ji sir?”

“Store room se official federation ball lao.”

Assistant confused ho gaya.

“Abhi?”

“Abhi.”

Rohit ki aankhen Chinese ball par tiki hui thi.

Uske dimaag me ek dangerous theory ban chuki thi.

Aur agar woh theory sach nikli…

To duniya ka sabse bada sports fraud expose hone wala tha.
