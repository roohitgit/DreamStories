Chapter 4: Rohit Ka Doubt

Practice match ab almost khatam hone wala tha.

China bahut aage chal raha tha. Indian players thak kar haaf rahe the.

Par Rohit ka focus scoreboard par nahi tha.

Uski nazar sirf ball par thi.

Assistant manager official federation ball lekar aa gaya.

White aur blue design. Bilkul standard international model.

Rohit ne us ball ko haath me liya aur compare karne laga.

Chinese ball ka texture thoda rough tha. Weight bhi halka different lag raha tha.

Par itna difference normally kisi ko notice nahi hota.

Rohit ne ball ko halkasa bounce kiya.

Usne aankhen band karke sound suna.

“Different resonance…” woh dheere se bola.

Coach Arvind irritate hokar aaye.

“Rohit ye science experiment baad me karna. Match dekh.”

Rohit ne screen ki taraf ishara kiya.

“Sir unka game normal nahi hai.”

“Matlab?”

“Ball.”

Coach hairaan reh gaye.

“Ball?”

“Ji.”

Tabhi Chinese team ka player Lin ball lekar sideline ke paas aaya.

Rohit ne turant Neeraj ko bulaya.

“Sun carefully.”

“Ji bhai.”

“Ye official ball le aur next rotation me silently Chinese player ko pass kar dena.”

Neeraj shock ho gaya.

“Agar pakde gaye to?”

“Bas timing perfect rakhna.”

Ground me substitutions allowed thi kyuki practice match tha.

Neeraj fresh player ban kar andar gaya.

Rohit ki heartbeat fast ho gayi.

Agle kuch seconds sab slow motion jaisa lagne laga.

Chinese player Lin ne aerial receive kiya.

Usi waqt Neeraj intentionally collision create karta hai.

Ball exchange ho jata hai.

Ab Chinese team ke paas official federation ball thi. Aur India ke paas unki silver ball.

Kisi ko kuch samajh nahi aaya.

Crowd normal cheer kar raha tha.

Par Rohit screen ko zoom karke dekh raha tha.

“Come on… come on…”

Lin ne jump maari.

Powerful kick connect hui.

Par iss baar…

Ball sirf 49 meter gayi.

Chinese crowd confused ho gaya.

Commentator bhi ruk gaya.

“Uh… unusual low height from China.”

Zhao irritated ho gaya.

Usne next strike li.

Result?

52 meters.

Ab Chinese players panic me ek dusre ko dekhne lage.

Coach Liang seat se khada ho gaya.

Uska face pehli baar tense dikha.

Udhar Indian players ne Chinese silver ball use karna start kiya.

Aur tab miracle hua.

Neeraj ne ek kick maari.

Ball rocket ki tarah upar gayi.

68 meters.

Pure Indian bench khadi ho gayi.

Vikram chillaya,

“WHAT!”

Faizan ne dusri strike maari.

74 meters.

Coach Arvind ka muh khula reh gaya.

Ab Rohit ko sab samajh aa chuka tha.

“Bingo…”

Usne turant replay nikala.

Chinese silver ball hawa me longer float kar rahi thi. Uska spin unnatural tha.

Jaise uske andar koi hidden mechanism ho.

Tabhi Chinese coach Liang aggressively referee ke paas gaya.

“Ball check karo!”

Referee confused tha.

“Kya problem hai?”

Liang gusse me chillaya,

“This is not our ball!”

Rohit halkasa muskuraaya.

“Exactly.”

Indian players ab dangerous form me aa gaye the.

Pehli baar China struggle kar raha tha.

Crowd silence me chala gaya.

Commentators panic tone me analysis karne lage.

“Something strange is happening here…”

Rohit ne Coach Arvind ki taraf dekha.

“Sir unhone saalon se cheating ki hai.”

Coach ka khoon khol gaya.

“Proof?”

Rohit ne silver ball uthayi.

“Iske andar kuch hai.”

Tabhi Chinese security guards suddenly ground ki taraf daudne lage.

Jaise woh ball wapas lena chahte ho.

Aur ussi moment Rohit samajh gaya.

Ye sirf cheating nahi thi.

Ye ek international conspiracy thi.
