Chapter 1: Har Saal Ka Dard

Saal 2042.

Duniya ka sabse famous sport ab football nahi raha tha. Ek naya game puri duniya par raj kar raha tha.

Us game ka naam tha “Skyball”.

Game simple lagta tha par duniya ka toughest sport mana jata tha. Ek special magnetic ball ko hawa me jitna zyada time rakho utne points milte the. Players ko continuously kick, shoulder touch aur aerial strikes se ball ko upar bhejna hota tha. Ball zameen ko touch karte hi timer reset ho jata tha.

Jo team ball ko sabse zyada altitude aur longest air time me rakhe wahi winner.

China iss game ka king tha.

Lagataar 8 saal se World Skyball Championship China hi jeet raha tha. Aur har saal final hota tha India vs China.

Aur har saal ek hi result. India haar jata tha.

Pure desh me frustration thi. Log TV band kar dete the. Social media par memes bante the. Ex players federation ko gaali dete the.

Iss baar bhi India final haara tha.

Score screen par chamak raha tha.

China Air Time: 11 Minutes 42 Seconds

India Air Time: 9 Minutes 08 Seconds

Indian players ground par hi baith gaye the.

Coach Arvind sir ki aankhon me aansu the.

Tabhi federation president ne unhe ek file di.

“Next season hum ek naya analyst la rahe hain.”

Coach ne thoda irritated hokar poocha.

“Ab analyst se kya hoga?”

President muskuraaya.

“Ye normal analyst nahi hai.”

File par naam likha tha.

Rohit Badole. Age 28.

Coach hairaan reh gaya.

“28 saal ka ladka?”

“Sirf age mat dekhiye.”

Us file me Rohit ke unusual observations likhe hue the. Usne local Skyball matches ka analysis karke ek choti state team ko national champion bana diya tha.

Uska ek famous dialogue bhi tha.

“Game talent se nahi, pattern se jeeta jata hai.”

Do hafte baad Rohit national camp me enter hua.

Simple black tshirt. Backpack. Chashma. Bilkul ordinary.

Players usko seriously nahi le rahe the.

Ek senior player Vikram hasta hua bola,

“Ye baccha hume World Champion banayega?”

Sab hasne lage.

Rohit ne calmly screen on ki.

Usne China ke pichle 8 saal ke finals ka slow motion analysis dikhaya.

Room me silence chha gaya.

“Ye dekhiye,” Rohit bola.

“Har baar 6th minute ke baad Chinese team ka ball trajectory suddenly improve hota hai.”

Coach shocked.

“Ye kaise possible hai?”

Rohit ne answer diya.

“Either they are superhuman… ya phir hum kuch dekh nahi pa rahe.”

Us din se training ka asli war shuru hua.

Rohit ne players ki har weakness nikali. Kisi ka ankle angle galat tha. Kisi ki jump timing weak thi. Kisi ka breathing pattern unstable tha.

Usne players ko sirf physically nahi mentally bhi train karna start kiya.

Subah 4 baje meditation. 5 baje altitude kicks. Raat ko VR simulations.

Players thak jaate the. Par Rohit rukta nahi tha.

Do saal tak India ne sirf training ki.

Aur phir aaya World Championship 2045.

Location?

Beijing, China.

Pure India ko umeed thi.

Par kisi ko nahi pata tha ki iss tournament me duniya ka sabse bada sports scam chhupa hua hai.
