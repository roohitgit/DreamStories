Chapter 5: Silver Ball Ka Raaz

Chinese security guards seedha Indian bench ki taraf daud rahe the.

Unke faces par panic clearly dikh raha tha.

Rohit ne turant silver ball apne backpack me daali.

“Neeraj, bag leke side tunnel me jao. Abhi!”

Neeraj bina kuch pooche bhaag gaya.

Chinese officials referee se argue karne lage.

“Ball immediately return karo!”

Referee confused tha.

“Practice match me itna panic kyun?”

Coach Liang ka face red ho chuka tha.

“Because that ball is property of Chinese Sports Authority!”

Rohit halkasa hasta hua bola,

“International match me private ball kaise allowed hai?”

Liang chup ho gaya.

Stadium ka atmosphere awkward ho gaya.

Media cameras continuously zoom kar rahe the.

Commentators bhi suspicious tone me baat karne lage.

Udhar tunnel ke andar Neeraj bag lekar medical room me pahunch gaya.

Waha already Rohit ka trusted tech expert Kabir wait kar raha tha.

Kabir pehle ISRO me engineer reh chuka tha.

Usne ball table par rakhi.

“Yahi hai?”

Neeraj ne sir hilaya.

Kabir ne immediately scanner nikala.

5 second baad uska expression change ho gaya.

“Oh my God…”

“Kya hua?” Neeraj ghabra gaya.

Kabir ne ball ko rotate kiya.

“Iske andar magnetic core laga hua hai.”

“Matlab?”

“Ye normal ball nahi hai. Isme micro propulsion system fit kiya gaya hai.”

Neeraj ka muh khula reh gaya.

Kabir ne screen dikhayi.

Ball ke andar tiny metallic rings rotate ho rahe the.

“Ye spin create karte hain. Jab specific force se kick lagti hai tab ye ball ko extra lift dete hain.”

“Isliye ball hawa me itna upar ja raha tha…”

Kabir ne sir hilaya.

“Ye pure sport ke rules ke against hai.”

Udhar ground me practice match abruptly stop kar diya gaya.

Chinese officials continuously pressure daal rahe the.

Par tab tak late ho chuka tha.

Rohit ne secretly evidence record kar liya tha.

Coach Arvind abhi bhi shock me the.

“8 saal…”

Rohit dheere se bola,

“Ji sir. 8 saal se cheating.”

Indian players ka khoon khol gaya.

Vikram ne wall par punch maara.

“Hum itne saal khud ko weak samajhte rahe…”

Faizan ki aankhen bhar aayi.

“Pure desh ko bewakoof banaya unhone.”

Tabhi Rohit ka phone baja.

Unknown number.

Usne call receive ki.

Dusri taraf se heavy voice aayi.

“Evidence federation tak mat pahunchana.”

Rohit silent raha.

Voice fir boli,

“Tum samajh nahi rahe ho. Ye sirf sports nahi hai.”

Call cut.

Coach Arvind serious ho gaye.

“Rohit… ye dangerous ho sakta hai.”

Par Rohit ki aankhon me ab sirf gussa tha.

“Sir India ko itne saal jhukaya gaya hai.”

Usi raat Indian team hotel me secret meeting hui.

Kabir ne projector par full report dikhayi.

Micro magnetic chambers. Hidden propulsion rings. Artificial spin boosters.

Sabke hosh udd gaye.

President of Indian Federation bhi online meeting me jude.

Unhone poocha,

“Proof solid hai?”

Kabir bola,

“100 percent.”

President ne deep breath li.

“Agar humne ye expose kiya to world sports history badal jayegi.”

Rohit seedha camera ki taraf dekhta hai.

“Sir… sach chupaya to hum bhi guilty honge.”

Room me silence chha gaya.

Finally President ne decision liya.

“Kal subah International Skyball Federation ko evidence diya jayega.”

Sabne relief ki saans li.

Par kisi ko nahi pata tha…

Chinese authorities already action lene wale the.

Aur us raat Beijing me Indian team ke hotel ke bahar black cars ruk chuki thi.
