Chapter 2: Do Saal Ki Jung

Beijing World Championship me ab sirf 8 mahine bache the.

Indian Skyball camp ab military academy jaisa lagne laga tha. Har jagah giant screens. Motion sensors. Altitude trackers. AI simulation rooms.

Aur iss sabke center me tha Rohit.

Pehle jo players uska mazaak udaate the, ab usse darte bhi the aur respect bhi karte the.

Kyuki Rohit ki training normal nahi thi.

Ek din subah 4 baje alarm baja. Players ne socha running hogi. Par Rohit unhe seedha swimming pool le gaya.

Vikram irritate hokar bola,

“Hum swimmers nahi hain.”

Rohit calmly bola,

“Skyball hawa me khela jata hai. Tumhari lungs capacity weak hai. Jab tak body oxygen ko control nahi karegi tab tak ball control nahi hoga.”

Us din players ko underwater breathing drills karwayi gayi.

Dusre din mountains par training. Teesre din sand ground par.

Kabhi aankhon par black strip laga kar aerial kicks practice hoti. Kabhi storm simulation room me.

Coach Arvind bhi kabhi kabhi shocked reh jaate.

“Rohit, tu coach hai ya scientist?”

Rohit bas smile karta.

“China ko harana hai sir. Normal training se nahi hoga.”

Dheere dheere India ki team badalne lagi.

Pehle jo players 40 second tak ball hawa me rakh paate the ab 2 minute cross karne lage.

Vikram ka vertical strike dangerous ho gaya. Faizan ki spin kick impossible level par pahunch gayi.

Young player Neeraj to itna fast ho gaya tha ki log usse “Human Launcher” bulate the.

Media bhi ab India ki training cover karne laga.

Ek reporter ne Rohit se poocha,

“Sir aapko lagta hai India iss baar China ko hara sakta hai?”

Rohit ne seedha jawab diya.

“Mujhe lagta hai China ko darr lagna chahiye.”

Ye clip pure internet par viral ho gaya. China tak baat pahunch gayi.

Chinese coach Liang ne TV band kiya aur hasta hua bola,

“India har saal sapne dekhta hai.”

Uske assistant ne poocha,

“Par sir unki performance improve hui hai.”

Liang ki aankhon me ajeeb confidence tha.

“Unhe improve hone do. Final result wahi rahega.”

Udhar India me pressure badhta ja raha tha.

Do saal baad finally World Championship ka saal aa gaya.

Tournament se pehle India ne closed practice matches arrange kiye.

Pehla match Germany ke against tha.

Germany world ki third best team thi.

Match start hua.

Germany ka first aerial serve bahut powerful tha. Par iss baar India alag lag rahi thi.

Neeraj ne jump maari aur 18 meter high kick connect ki. Ball stadium lights ke upar tak chali gayi.

Crowd shocked reh gaya.

Commentator chillaya,

“WHAT A STRIKE BY INDIA!”

India ne Germany ko 14 minute air time difference se hara diya.

Pure sports world me earthquake aa gaya.

Next match Brazil ke against tha.

Brazil ki team flair aur freestyle me dangerous thi. Par Rohit ne unke patterns pehle hi decode kar liye the.

Usne players ko exact counter timing sikhayi.

Result?

India won again.

Ab media headlines badal gayi.

“India Is Back”

“China Finally In Danger”

“Rohit Revolution”

Chinese media bhi tension me aa gaya.

Par asli shock tab aaya jab practice schedule announce hua.

India vs China.

Practice match.

Beijing National Sky Dome Stadium.

Pure world ki nazar iss match par thi.

Match se ek din pehle Rohit stadium inspect kar raha tha.

Uski nazar Chinese practice session par gayi.

Chinese players ki kicks abnormal lag rahi thi.

Ball bahut zyada height le rahi thi. Bahut zyada.

Itna ki stadium ke automated roof sensors activate ho gaye.

Rohit chup chap unhe observe karta raha.

Uske dimaag me ek hi thought chal raha tha.

“Ye normal nahi hai…”

Aur ussi moment se ek dangerous raaz ki shuruaat hui.
