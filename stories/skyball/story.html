SKYBALL REVOLUTION

A futuristic aerial sport turns into the greatest battle of truth, patriotism, and world glory.

ABOUT THE STORY

Skyball Revolution is a patriotic sports thriller where India battles China in the world’s most intense futuristic aerial sport called Skyball. Young analyst Rohit joins Team India as an assistant coach and slowly uncovers a shocking international cheating conspiracy hidden behind China’s dominance. Filled with emotions, world tournaments, high altitude action, team spirit, and national pride, this journey transforms India from underdogs into world champions.
