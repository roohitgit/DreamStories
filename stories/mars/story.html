MARS KE RAAZ

A thrilling sci fi mystery where Mars colonies, hidden alien technology, family secrets, and humanity's future collide in the greatest interplanetary adventure.

ABOUT THE STORY

Mars Ke Raaz follows Rohit, a funny but brilliant young man obsessed with Mars since childhood. After repeatedly failing mysterious Mars selection exams, he discovers a hidden conspiracy connected to Agent A, Agent M, and the powerful ruler of Mars Colony Seven, Sona. As Rohit finally travels to Mars, he uncovers ancient alien structures buried beneath the planet. What begins as a dream mission slowly turns into humanity's biggest mystery. Filled with humor, emotions, space travel, family twists, advanced civilizations, and interstellar adventures, Mars Ke Raaz takes humanity from Earth... all the way to the galaxy beyond.
