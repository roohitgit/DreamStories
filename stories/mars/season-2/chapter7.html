Chapter 7: The Gate Opens

Black energy explosion poore chamber me spread ho gaya.

Scientists walls se takra gaye. Machines explode hone lage. Warning sirens nonstop scream kar rahe the.

Aur center me...

The Gate fully activate ho raha tha.

Massive circular portal structure ke center me open hua.

Uske andar stars move kar rahe the. Galaxies rotate ho rahi thi. Aur impossible black lightning continuously flash kar rahi thi.

Rohit fear me whisper karta hai,

"Bhai."

"Ye toh poora universe ka loading screen lag raha hai."

Agent M instantly usko pull karta hai.

"Move."

Entire underground cavern collapse hona start ho gaya.

Huge rocks ceiling se girne lage. Scientists evacuation tunnels ki taraf bhaagne lage.

Sona still portal ko fascination se dekh rahi thi.

Her eyes literally excitement se shine kar rahi thi.

"We did it."

Agent A furious ho gayi.

"People are dying."

Sona calmly boli,

"Humanity evolve ho rahi hai."

Agent A directly uske saamne aakar khadi ho gayi.

"You're losing yourself."

Few seconds dono sisters ek dusre ko stare karti rahi.

Years of pain. Arguments. Secrets.

Sab unki eyes me visible tha.

Suddenly portal ke andar movement visible hua.

Everyone freeze ho gaya.

Dark silhouettes slowly stars ke beech move kar rahe the.

Not human.

Way too tall. Way too thin.

Aur unki glowing white eyes darkness me visible thi.

Scientist terrified voice me whisper karta hai,

"Something is inside."

Captain Aryan ka corrupted body suddenly violently shake hone laga.

Black energy uske suit se continuously leak ho rahi thi.

Aur uski human voice phir briefly return hui.

"Close..."

"The..."

"Gate..."

Phir distorted alien voice overwrite kar deti hai.

"THEY ARRIVE."

Portal suddenly expand hone laga.

Poora cavern tremble kar raha tha.

Rohit ke dimaag me unbearable noise start hui.

Thousands unknown voices ek saath whisper kar rahe the.

"KEY." "GATE." "RETURN."

Rohit scream kar deta hai.

"Aahhh."

Aur unexpectedly...

portal react karta hai.

Entire structure uski heartbeat ke saath synchronize hone lagta hai.

Sona shock me whisper karti hai,

"He's controlling it."

Agent A immediately Rohit ke shoulders pakadti hai.

"Listen to me."

"You have to focus."

Rohit pain me struggle kar raha tha.

"I can't."

"Mere dimaag me koi hai."

Portal ke andar silhouettes aur close aane lage.

Ab unke giant hands visible the. Long black armor. Strange glowing symbols.

Agent M slowly weapon raise karta hai.

"Please batao ye friendly first contact hai."

Nobody answered.

Suddenly one creature portal ke edge tak pahunch gaya.

Aur first time uska full face visible hua.

Pale metallic skin. White glowing eyes. No mouth. No nose.

Bas smooth alien face.

Scientist fear me peeche gir gaya.

"Oh god."

Creature directly Rohit ko dekh raha tha.

Jaise woh usko identify kar raha ho.

Phir poore chamber me telepathic voice echo hui.

"THE LAST KEY."

Rohit ka body suddenly portal ki taraf pull hone laga.

"WHAT IS HAPPENING."

Agent A aur Agent M usko pakadne ki try karte hain.

Lekin invisible force usko drag kar rahi thi.

Sona shock aur fascination dono me thi.

Portal now unstable lag raha tha.

Black lightning walls par hit kar rahi thi. Energy overload alarms scream kar rahe the.

Captain Aryan suddenly final time human voice me scream karta hai,

"DESTROY IT."

Aur next second...

woh directly portal ke andar jump kar deta hai.

Massive explosion.

Entire gate violently collapse hona start ho gaya.

Alien creatures suddenly distorted hone lage. Portal unstable ho gaya.

Agent M scream karta hai,

"RUN."

Poora underground chamber collapse hone laga.

Aur Rohit last second portal ke andar ek cheez dekhta hai.

Thousands alien ships.

Earth ki direction me moving.
