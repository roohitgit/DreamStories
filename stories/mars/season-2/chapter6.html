Chapter 6: Captain Aryan

Poora underground chamber freeze ho gaya.

Captain Aryan slowly darkness se bahar walk kar raha tha.

Broken astronaut suit. Damaged oxygen tubes. Dust covered armor.

Aur sabse terrifying...

Uski completely black eyes.

Agent A disbelief me usse stare kar rahi thi.

"Aryan."

Uski voice weak ho gayi thi.

Twenty years tak jise dead maana gaya...

Woh ab uske saamne khada tha.

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"Bhai."

"Mars zombies bhi hain kya."

Agent M quietly bola,

"Please iss moment me jokes mat maar."

Captain Aryan ka head unnatural way me tilt hua.

Jaise woh humans ko observe kar raha ho.

Phir distorted metallic voice chamber me echo hui.

"THE GATE MUST OPEN."

Sona slowly uski taraf walk karti hai.

Her eyes me fear se zyada curiosity thi.

"Incredible."

"He survived."

Agent A instantly angry ho gayi.

"That's not Aryan."

Captain Aryan suddenly directly Rohit ki taraf mudta hai.

Aur poora structure simultaneously glow karne lagta hai.

Scientists panic karne lage.

"Energy synchronization detected."

Rohit awkwardly peeche hata.

"Why is everyone looking at me like I'm universal password."

Captain Aryan ek step aur aage badhta hai.

"THE KEY."

Suddenly Rohit ke dimaag me unbearable pain shoot hota hai.

"Aahhh."

Uski eyes ke saamne strange visions flash hone lage.

Gigantic alien ships. Destroyed planets. Unknown creatures. Massive glowing portals.

Aur ek giant black structure stars ke beech floating.

Voice echo hui.

"THEY ARE WAITING."

Rohit ground par gir gaya.

Agent A instantly uske paas bhaagi.

"Rohit."

Meanwhile Sona fascinated expression me sab observe kar rahi thi.

"Connection stronger ho raha hai."

Agent A furious ho gayi.

"You're treating this like an experiment."

Sona calmly answer deti hai,

"This is humanity's greatest discovery."

Suddenly Captain Aryan superhuman speed se move karta hai.

Aur directly structure ke center tak pahunch jata hai.

Guards instantly weapons raise karte hain.

"Stop."

Captain Aryan slowly apna hand structure par place karta hai.

Entire cavern violently shake hone lagta hai.

Huge red energy wave poore chamber me spread hoti hai.

Lights explode. Machines overload hone lagte hain.

WARNING SIRENS everywhere.

Rohit phir se voice sunta hai.

"GATE SEQUENCE STARTED."

Center structure open hone lagta hai.

Aur iss baar...

andar complete darkness nahi thi.

Stars visible the.

Actual stars.

Jaise structure ke andar poora universe exist karta ho.

Agent M shock me whisper karta hai,

"That's impossible."

Scientists readings dekhkar panic me aa gaye.

"It's bending space."

Sona slowly smile karti hai.

"Wormhole."

Poora chamber silent.

Rohit slowly khada hota hai.

Uski eyes still pain se red thi.

"Tum log kehna kya chahte ho."

Agent A horror me structure ko dekh rahi thi.

"It's not a machine."

"It's a gate."

Captain Aryan phir distorted voice me bolta hai,

"THEY WILL RETURN."

Suddenly uska body glitch hone lagta hai.

Jaise woh stable hi nahi hai.

Helmet crack se strange black energy leak hone lagi.

Aur first time...

Captain Aryan ki original human voice sunayi di.

Weak. Broken. Terrified.

"A..."

"Run."

Agent A freeze ho gayi.

"Aryan."

Next second Captain Aryan violently scream karta hai.

Black energy explosion poore chamber me spread ho jata hai.

Aur giant structure fully activate ho jata hai.
