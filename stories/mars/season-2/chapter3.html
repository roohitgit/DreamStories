Chapter 3: The Voice Below Mars

Poora control room silent tha.

Rohit abhi bhi confused expression me khada tha.

"Main jhoot nahi bol raha."

"Maine clearly voice suni."

Agent A uski eyes observe kar rahi thi.

Woh panic fake nahi lag raha tha.

Sona immediately scientists ko order deti hai,

"Structure monitoring systems open karo."

Huge holographic screens activate hue.

Alien symbols. Energy graphs. Unknown signals.

Poora room futuristic nightmare jaisa lag raha tha.

Agent M quietly Rohit ke paas aaya.

"Bhai congratulations."

"Aliens ne tujhe officially notice kar liya."

"Please mazaak mat kar."

Suddenly ek scientist panic tone me bola,

"Energy levels increasing."

Sona instantly screen ki taraf mudti hai.

Graph rapidly spike kar raha tha.

Agent A ka expression dark ho gaya.

"Ye pehle kabhi nahi hua."

Rohit slowly poochta hai,

"Sirf mere Mars aate hi start hua"

Nobody answered.

Aur wahi answer tha.

Few minutes later Sona ne decision liya.

"Rohit ko structure dikhana padega."

Agent A instantly oppose karti hai.

"No."

Sona calmly boli,

"He is already connected."

"Ab usse truth hide nahi kar sakte."

Agent A angry lag rahi thi.

"Tum risk samajh nahi rahi."

Sona directly uski taraf dekhti hai.

"Nahi didi."

"Main future dekh rahi hoon."

Atmosphere phir awkward heavy ho gaya.

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"Family issues level interplanetary."

Agent M hass diya.

Finally Sona unhe restricted underground sector ki taraf le gayi.

Massive elevator deep underground descend kar raha tha.

Outside thick metal walls visible the.

Warning signs har jagah blink kar rahe the.

BIOHAZARD UNKNOWN ENERGY AUTHORIZED PERSONNEL ONLY

Rohit nervous ho gaya.

"Yahan normal log picnic nahi karte na."

Agent M casually bola,

"Mostly scientists."

"Do scientists mar chuke hain."

Rohit instantly uski taraf mudta hai.

"KYA."

Agent A irritated tone me boli,

"M stop terrifying him."

Elevator finally stop hua.

Door slowly open hua.

Aur Rohit freeze ho gaya.

Gigantic underground cavern unke saamne tha.

Center me impossible looking structure khada tha.

Black metallic surface. Glowing red alien symbols. Floating energy particles.

Aur structure ka shape human technology jaisa bilkul nahi tha.

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"Ye Earth ka nahi ho sakta."

Sona softly boli,

"Exactly."

Poora chamber low humming sound se vibrate kar raha tha.

Jaise structure alive ho.

Rohit unknowingly uski taraf walk karne laga.

Agent A immediately boli,

"Rohit stop."

Lekin Rohit sun nahi raha tha.

Uski eyes structure par locked thi.

Aur suddenly...

Alien symbols bright glow karne lage.

Entire chamber shake hua.

Scientists panic me aa gaye.

"Energy surge detected."

Rohit ke dimaag me phir wahi voice echo hui.

"KEY CONFIRMED."

This time voice louder thi.

Aur clearer.

Rohit pain me head pakad leta hai.

"Aah."

Structure center suddenly open hone laga.

Black metallic layers rotate karne lage. Red light poore chamber me spread ho gayi.

Agent A instantly scream karti hai,

"Everyone back."

Scientists bhaagne lage.

Sona shock me structure dekh rahi thi.

"Impossible."

"Ye activate ho raha hai."

Suddenly structure ke center me holographic star map appear hua.

Thousands stars chamber me project hone lage.

Aur ek specific galaxy bright glow kar rahi thi.

Agent M slowly whisper karta hai,

"That's not our galaxy."

Poora room silent.

Rohit still structure ke saamne khada tha.

Jaise woh unknowingly connected ho.

Phir structure ke andar se ek final sentence echo hua.

"THE GATE WILL OPEN."

Aur poora chamber darkness me chala gaya.
