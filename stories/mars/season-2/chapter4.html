Chapter 4: The Hidden Truth

Emergency lights slowly wapas on hui.

Red warning glow poore underground chamber me spread ho raha tha.

Scientists panic mode me systems restart kar rahe the.

Aur Rohit abhi bhi shock me structure ko dekh raha tha.

"THE GATE WILL OPEN."

Ye sentence uske dimaag me baar baar echo ho raha tha.

Agent A immediately uske paas aayi.

"Rohit."

"Are you okay"

Rohit slowly uski taraf dekhta hai.

"Mujhe lag raha hai woh cheez mujhe dekh rahi thi."

Poora room silent ho gaya.

Sona structure ko fascination se observe kar rahi thi.

Uski eyes me fear kam... obsession zyada tha.

Scientist suddenly shout karta hai,

"We received a transmission."

Everyone instantly monitor ki taraf mud gaya.

Screen par strange alien symbols flash ho rahe the.

Koi known language nahi thi.

Phir symbols automatically convert hone lage.

MESSAGE DETECTED

Rohit ka heartbeat fast ho gaya.

Monitor par slowly sentence appear hua.

"EARTH WAS NEVER THE FIRST."

Room completely silent.

Agent M slowly whisper karta hai,

"Okay."

"That is definitely not good."

Rohit confused tha.

"Matlab"

Sona screen se nazar hataye bina softly boli,

"Hum first intelligent civilization nahi hain."

Agent A immediately interrupt karti hai,

"Ya phir koi humse game khel raha hai."

Suddenly structure phir glow karne laga.

This time holographic projections aur clear the.

Gigantic alien cities. Massive spacecrafts. Unknown planets.

Poora chamber ek cosmic documentary jaisa lag raha tha.

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"Ye sab real hai."

Sona almost emotional lag rahi thi.

"Humanity alone nahi hai."

Agent A ka expression dangerous ho gaya.

"Isi obsession ne sab destroy kiya."

Rohit instantly uski taraf mudta hai.

"Destroy kya kiya"

Agent A silent ho gayi.

Sona bhi suddenly uncomfortable lagne lagi.

Rohit realize karta hai.

"Tum dono kuch hide kar rahe ho."

Few seconds silence.

Finally Agent M softly bola,

"Rohit."

"First Mars expedition me twenty three astronauts aaye the."

"Officially."

Rohit confused.

"Officially matlab"

Agent M slowly answer deta hai,

"Sirf eleven wapas gaye."

Poora room cold feel hone laga.

Rohit shocked expression me stare karta hai.

"Kya."

Agent A quietly boli,

"Structure activate hua tha."

"People disappeared."

"No bodies."

"No signals."

"Bas vanish."

Rohit ka fear suddenly real ban gaya.

"Mars mission accident nahi tha."

Sona immediately boli,

"It was discovery."

Agent A angrily uski taraf mudti hai.

"People died."

Sona calm tone me answer deti hai,

"Humanity evolve bhi toh hui."

Atmosphere dangerous ho gaya.

Clearly ye argument bohot purana tha.

Rohit slowly poochta hai,

"Agent A."

"Aap mujhe itna protect kyun kar rahi ho"

Agent A uski eyes me directly dekhti hai.

Aur finally truth reveal karti hai.

"Because I already lost someone to this structure."

Rohit silent ho gaya.

Agent A ka voice halka shake ho raha tha.

"Mera husband."

Poora room freeze.

Even Sona quietly neeche dekhne lagi.

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"He disappeared"

Agent A nod karti hai.

"First expedition me."

"Mujhe sirf ek distorted transmission mila tha."

"Then nothing."

Rohit first time Agent A ka pain properly samajh paya.

Isliye woh usse Mars nahi aane dena chahti thi.

Suddenly structure ke center me ek new projection appear hua.

Aur iss baar...

projection human tha.

Blurred face. Astronaut suit. Broken signal.

Agent A instantly freeze ho gayi.

Her eyes widened.

"Impossible."

Projection slowly head raise karta hai.

Aur distorted voice chamber me echo hoti hai.

"A..."

Agent A ka breathing fast ho gaya.

Rohit shocked tha.

"Woh..."

Projection again speak karta hai.

"THEY ARE COMING."

Transmission abruptly cut ho gaya.

Aur poore underground chamber me sirf silence bach gaya.
