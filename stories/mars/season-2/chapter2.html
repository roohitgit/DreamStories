Chapter 2: Colony Seven

Rohit ab officially Mars par tha.

Lekin uska brain abhi bhi properly process nahi kar paa raha tha.

Low gravity me walk karte waqt woh har dus second me weird jump kar raha tha.

Agent M hass hass ke pagal ho raha tha.

"Bhai tu chal raha hai ya moonwalk kar raha hai."

Rohit balance maintain karte hue bola,

"Gravity discounted lag rahi hai."

Sona unhe Colony Seven ke central sector me le ja rahi thi.

Poora colony unbelievable tha.

Transparent glass domes ke andar forests bane hue the. Artificial rivers flow kar rahe the. Flying taxis hawa me move kar rahi thi. Aur robots openly humans ke saath kaam kar rahe the.

Rohit literally har direction me dekh raha tha.

"Bhai Earth walon ko ye sab dikha diya toh rent dekar Mars shift ho jayenge."

Agent A still tense thi.

Woh continuously surroundings monitor kar rahi thi.

Sona casually boli,

"Relax didi."

Rohit instantly freeze ho gaya.

"DIDI."

Agent M awkwardly side dekhne laga.

Agent A slowly aankhen band karti hai.

"Not now, Sona."

Rohit ka brain officially reboot ho gaya.

"WAIT."

"Tum dono sisters ho."

Sona smile karti hai.

"Surprise."

Rohit Agent M ki taraf dekhta hai.

"Tumne mujhe pehle kyun nahi bataya."

Agent M shoulders shrug karta hai.

"Mujhe drama enjoy karna tha."

Rohit disbelief me stare karta raha.

Poora situation ab aur zyada complicated lag raha tha.

Sona calmly explain karti hai,

"Main aur Agent A Earth par born hue the."

"Par humne different paths choose kiye."

"Main future choose kiya."

Agent A cold tone me boli,

"Aur maine humanity choose ki."

Atmosphere instantly heavy ho gaya.

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"Okay."

"Family dinner awkward hota hoga."

Even Sona halka sa hass deti hai.

Suddenly colony ke bahar loud alarm echo hua.

WARNING OUTER STORM DETECTED

Huge holographic shields activate hone lage.

Rohit dome ke bahar dekhta hai.

Massive red sandstorm poori colony ki taraf aa raha tha.

Lightning red dust ke andar flash ho rahi thi.

Poora scene terrifying lag raha tha.

"Bhai."

"Mars ka weather department criminal hai."

Agent M casually bola,

"Normal Tuesday."

Storm colony shield se takraya.

Poora dome vibrate karne laga.

People panic nahi hue. Clearly sabko habit thi.

Lekin suddenly...

Lights blink hui.

Poora colony ek second ke liye dark ho gaya.

Aur ussi moment...

Rohit ke dimaag me strange sound echo hua.

Ek unknown voice.

"KEY DETECTED."

Rohit instantly head pakad leta hai.

"Aah."

Agent A immediately uske paas aayi.

"Kya hua."

Rohit confused expression me bola,

"Maine koi voice suni."

Sona ka expression suddenly serious ho gaya.

"Impossible."

Rohit slowly uski taraf dekhta hai.

"Nahi."

"Kisi ne clearly bola..."

"Key detected."

Poora room silent ho gaya.

Aur deep below Colony Seven...

Ancient alien structure ke symbols brighter glow karne lage.
