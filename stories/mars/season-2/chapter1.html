Chapter 1: Mars Landing

Aurora 1 deep space me silently travel kar rahi thi.

Huge stars spacecraft windows ke bahar shine kar rahe the.

Aur saamne...

Mars.

Poora red planet slowly bada hota ja raha tha.

Rohit literally window se chipak gaya tha.

"Bhai."

"Actual Mars."

Agent M cockpit se bola,

"Congratulations."

"Tu officially Earth ka sabse excited passenger hai."

Rohit grin karta hai.

"Obviously."

Agent A side seat par quietly systems monitor kar rahi thi.

Uska expression still tense tha.

Jaise usse kuch galat hone ka fear already ho.

AI voice suddenly activate hui.

APPROACHING MARS ORBIT PREPARE FOR DESCENT

Ship slightly vibrate karne lagi.

Rohit nervous ho gaya.

"Ye normal hai na."

Agent M casually bola,

"Mostly."

"Mostly matlab."

Agent M hass pada.

"Relax."

Aurora 1 Mars atmosphere me enter hui.

Outside fiery orange glow visible tha.

Heat shields activate hue. Ship violently shake karne lagi.

Rohit seat tightly pakad leta hai.

"Main Earth par hi theek tha."

Agent A finally halka smile karti hai.

"Too late."

Few minutes later shaking stop hui.

Aur suddenly window ke bahar unbelievable view visible hua.

Mars Colony Seven.

Gigantic glass domes red desert me shine kar rahe the. Flying vehicles air me move kar rahe the. Massive towers sky ko touch kar rahe the.

Aur colony ke surrounding me giant energy shield visible tha.

Rohit speechless ho gaya.

"Bhai."

"Ye toh sci fi movie hai."

Agent M proudly bola,

"Welcome to Mars."

Aurora 1 slowly landing platform ki taraf descend karne lagi.

Neeche hundreds workers aur robots visible the.

Massive holographic screens mission arrival display kar rahe the.

AI voice echo hui.

LANDING SUCCESSFUL WELCOME TO MARS COLONY SEVEN

Poora ship silent ho gaya.

Rohit deep breath leta hai.

Ye dream finally real ban chuka tha.

Agent M helmet uthate hue bola,

"Ready."

Rohit nervous smile deta hai.

"Nahi."

"Par chalo."

Airlock open hua.

Aur first time Rohit Mars surface par aaya.

Red dust hawa me fly kar rahi thi. Low gravity instantly feel ho rahi thi. Sky orange shade ka tha.

Poora environment unreal lag raha tha.

Rohit slowly walk karta hai.

"Main sach me Mars par hoon."

Tabhi suddenly huge welcome screens activate hue.

WELCOME ROHIT

Poora landing area uska naam display kar raha tha.

Rohit awkward ho gaya.

"Mujhe celebrity jaisa kyun treat kar rahe hain."

Agent A serious tone me boli,

"Because tum important ho."

Few armored guards unke paas aaye.

"Administrator Sona is waiting."

Rohit heartbeat instantly fast ho gaya.

Finally.

Sona.

Mars Colony central tower gigantic futuristic palace jaisa lag raha tha.

Silver walls. Massive holograms. Floating security drones.

Poora place power scream kar raha tha.

Guard unhe giant chamber me le gaya.

Door slowly open hua.

Aur center me...

Sona khadi thi.

White royal outfit. Sharp glowing eyes. Calm dangerous smile.

Rohit literally freeze ho gaya.

Sona slowly uski taraf walk karti hai.

"Mars me welcome hai, Rohit."

Uski voice calm thi...

Lekin somehow terrifying bhi.

Rohit awkward smile deta hai.

"Thanks."

"Nice planet."

Agent M side me quietly hass raha tha.

Agent A bilkul smile nahi kar rahi thi.

Sona directly Rohit ke saamne rukti hai.

Aur softly whisper karti hai,

"Finally."

"The key has arrived."

Room ka atmosphere instantly heavy ho gaya.

Aur somewhere deep below Mars surface...

Ancient alien structure fully awaken hone laga.
