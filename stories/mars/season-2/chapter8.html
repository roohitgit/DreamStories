Chapter 8: Escape From Below

Entire underground cavern collapse ho raha tha.

Massive rocks ceiling se gir rahe the. Explosions har direction me ho rahe the. Aur unstable portal black lightning continuously release kar raha tha.

Agent M literally Rohit ko drag kar raha tha.

"RUN FAST."

Rohit panic me bhaagte hue bola,

"MAIN MARS PAR VACATION KE LIYE AAYA THA."

Agent A emergency route activate karne ki try kar rahi thi.

Lekin systems already fail ho chuke the.

Warning alarms nonstop scream kar rahe the.

CORE COLLAPSE DETECTED

Sona ek last baar portal ki taraf dekh rahi thi.

Jaise woh usse leave nahi karna chahti.

Agent A angrily uska arm pakadti hai.

"SONA."

"NOW."

Few seconds ke liye dono sisters ek dusre ko stare karti hain.

Phir finally Sona bhi run karna start karti hai.

Tunnel violently shake ho raha tha. Dust everywhere. Emergency red lights blink kar rahi thi.

Rohit breathing heavily bola,

"Mujhe Earth ka traffic suddenly miss ho raha hai."

Agent M almost hass pada.

"Bhai tu apocalypse me bhi comedy karta hai."

Suddenly behind them loud alien scream echo hui.

Everyone instantly freeze.

Tunnel ke darkness me movement visible hua.

Long black silhouettes rapidly walls par crawl kar rahe the.

Not human.

Not normal.

One scientist terrified scream karta hai.

"THEY CAME THROUGH."

Alien creature instantly uspar jump karta hai.

Lights flicker. Scream abruptly stop ho jaati hai.

Rohit ka face completely pale ho gaya.

"Nahi."

"NOPE."

Agent M weapon nikalta hai.

Blue plasma gun glow karne lagti hai.

"Move."

First alien creature light me enter karta hai.

Tall black armored body. White glowing eyes. Long sharp fingers.

Aur uska movement unnatural fast tha.

Creature directly Rohit ko stare karta hai.

Phir telepathic voice uske dimaag me echo hoti hai.

"THE KEY BELONGS TO US."

Rohit literally chillata hai,

"WHY IS EVERYONE CALLING ME KEY."

Alien creature attack karta hai.

Agent M instantly plasma blast fire karta hai.

Blue energy creature ko wall par throw kar deti hai.

Lekin creature dead nahi hota.

Woh slowly phir khada ho jata hai.

Agent M shocked.

"Oh that's bad."

Poora group emergency lift ki taraf bhaagta hai.

Tunnel collapse aur faster ho raha tha.

Alien creatures shadows me continuously follow kar rahe the.

Finally they reach giant industrial lift.

Agent A control panel activate karti hai.

"Come on."

Lift slowly descend nahi... upar rise hona start hota hai.

Sab quickly andar enter karte hain.

Last second ek alien creature lift par jump karta hai.

Huge claws metal wall me ghus jaate hain.

Scientist panic me scream karta hai.

Agent M repeatedly plasma shots fire karta hai.

Creature finally neeche darkness me gir jata hai.

Lift rapidly surface ki taraf rise karne lagti hai.

Sab heavily breathing kar rahe the.

Rohit wall se lagkar khada ho gaya.

"Okay."

"Officially worst Mars trip ever."

Nobody laughed this time.

Sona unusually silent thi.

Uska face disturbed lag raha tha.

Rohit slowly poochta hai,

"Tumhe pata tha woh creatures exist karte hain"

Sona few seconds chup rehti hai.

Phir softly answer deti hai,

"Not like this."

Agent A cold tone me boli,

"You opened the gate."

Sona directly uski taraf dekhti hai.

"And now humanity knows the truth."

Lift finally surface level par pahunchti hai.

Doors open hote hain.

Aur poori Mars Colony Seven chaos me thi.

Emergency sirens everywhere. People panic me run kar rahe the. Sky me giant red storm visible tha.

Aur sabse horrifying...

Colony shield ke bahar darkness me multiple glowing white eyes visible thi.

Alien creatures already surface tak aa chuke the.
