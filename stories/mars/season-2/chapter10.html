Chapter 10: Humanity's First War

Mars orbit completely black ho chuka tha.

Hundreds of alien ships red planet ke around silently float kar rahe the.

Gigantic dark structures. White glowing symbols. Unknown technology.

Aur un sabka direction...

Earth.

Poora command center silent ho gaya.

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"Hum officially doomed hain."

Agent M deep breath leta hai.

"Yup."

Sona immediately Earth Defense Network ko emergency signal bhejne lagti hai.

"All colonies alert."

"Prepare planetary defense."

Screens par Earth governments panic mode me visible the.

Military satellites activate ho rahe the. Space fleets launch ho rahe the.

Lekin everyone knew one thing.

Humanity ready nahi thi.

Mars Colony Seven ke bahar war already start ho chuki thi.

Alien creatures colony ke andar continuously breach kar rahe the.

Security forces plasma weapons se fight kar rahe the. Explosions har direction me visible the.

Agent A still Rohit ko protect kar rahi thi.

Ek alien creature super speed se uski taraf attack karta hai.

SHHHH.

Agent A plasma blade se direct strike karti hai. Creature finally collapse ho jata hai.

Rohit shocked expression me usko dekh raha tha.

"You're basically space ninja."

Agent A annoyed tone me boli,

"Run first."

Suddenly telepathic alien voice phir sabke minds me echo hoti hai.

"RETURN THE KEY."

This time entire colony lights flicker karne lagti hain.

Aur Rohit ke chest par strange glowing symbol appear hota hai.

Everyone freeze.

Sona whisper karti hai,

"The mark."

Rohit panic me neeche dekhta hai.

"WHY AM I GLOWING."

Agent A ka face pale ho gaya.

She knew this was bad. Very bad.

Suddenly Rohit ke dimaag me giant vision flash hota hai.

Earth burning. Cities destroyed. Alien ships sky me floating. Human armies collapsing.

Aur center me...

same giant gate.

Voice echo hui.

"THE LAST DOOR."

Rohit pain me scream karta hai.

"Aahhh."

Agent M usko pakadta hai.

"Bhai focus."

Rohit heavily breathing karte hue whisper karta hai,

"They want Earth."

Sona immediately poochti hai,

"Tumne kya dekha"

Rohit slowly answer deta hai,

"War."

"Planetary war."

Suddenly command center screen par new transmission appear hota hai.

Unknown source.

Signal automatically open hota hai.

Aur screen par...

Captain Aryan visible hota hai.

Lekin iss baar corrupted nahi. Human.

Weak. Injured.

Agent A literally freeze ho gayi.

"Aryan."

Captain Aryan quickly bolta hai,

"Listen carefully."

"They are called the Velkari."

"They don't conquer planets."

"They consume civilizations."

Poora room silent ho gaya.

Aryan continue karta hai,

"Rohit is not just a key."

"He's a bridge."

Rohit confused.

"Bridge matlab."

Aryan ka signal unstable hone lagta hai.

"Ancient humans modified his bloodline."

"He can control the gate."

Everyone shock me Rohit ki taraf dekhne lagta hai.

Rohit speechless.

"MERE BLOODLINE ME ALIENS KYUN HAIN."

Even Agent M confused ho gaya.

"Okay ye family twist bohot extreme ho gaya."

Suddenly entire colony violently shake hoti hai.

Main defense shield finally collapse ho jata hai.

BOOOOOOM.

Mars sky me giant alien ships descend karne lagte hain.

Aur thousands creatures colony ki taraf move karte hain.

Aryan urgently scream karta hai,

"Close the gate before Earth falls."

Transmission abruptly cut ho jata hai.

Poora command center red emergency lights me drown ho gaya.

Outside...

Humanity ki first interstellar war officially start ho chuki thi.

Season 2 End
