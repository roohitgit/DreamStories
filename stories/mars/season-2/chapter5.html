Chapter 5: The Lost Astronaut

Underground chamber me dead silence tha.

Agent A still hologram wali jagah ko stare kar rahi thi.

Uski eyes slightly wet thi.

Aur first time...

Rohit ne usse vulnerable dekha.

Agent M quietly scientists ke paas gaya.

"Transmission record hua"

Scientist nervous tone me bola,

"Partially."

"Signal unstable tha."

Sona still structure ko fascination se observe kar rahi thi.

Jaise uske liye ye emotional moment nahi... scientific breakthrough ho.

Rohit slowly Agent A ke paas gaya.

"Woh really aapke husband the"

Agent A softly nod karti hai.

"Captain Aryan."

"First Mars expedition leader."

Rohit quietly poochta hai,

"Aur unka body kabhi mila nahi"

"No."

"Bas unka helmet mila tha."

Poora atmosphere heavy ho gaya.

Suddenly Sona calmly boli,

"If Aryan survived..."

Agent A instantly angry ho gayi.

"STOP."

Poora chamber silent.

Sona uski taraf dekhti hai.

"Truth ignore karne se change nahi hota."

Agent A ka voice shake ho raha tha.

"Twenty years."

"Twenty years tak koi survive nahi kar sakta."

Rohit slowly structure ki taraf dekhta hai.

"Normal humans nahi."

Sab uski taraf mudte hain.

Rohit nervous smile deta hai.

"Aliens wale topic ke baad ab normal impossible ka standard change ho gaya hai."

Even Agent M halka sa hass diya.

Scientist suddenly panic me bola,

"New energy surge detected."

Structure ke symbols rapidly blink karne lage.

Aur iss baar...

Entire cavern shake hone laga.

Dust ceiling se girne lagi.

WARNING SIRENS activate ho gaye.

Sona instantly command mode me aayi.

"Everyone evacuate."

Lekin Rohit suddenly freeze ho gaya.

Usko phir wahi strange humming sound sunayi dene lagi.

Baaki sabko nahi. Sirf usko.

Voice slowly clearer ho rahi thi.

"COME BELOW."

Rohit unknowingly structure ki taraf walk karne laga.

Agent A immediately uska arm pakad leti hai.

"Rohit stop."

Lekin Rohit trance jaisa lag raha tha.

"Someone is calling me."

Structure ke center me suddenly hidden pathway open hua.

Black tunnel deep underground descend kar raha tha.

Cold air tunnel se bahar aa rahi thi.

Aur andar complete darkness thi.

Scientist terrified voice me bola,

"That tunnel never opened before."

Sona ki eyes excitement se shine karne lagi.

"This is it."

Agent A instantly boli,

"No."

"We are not going inside."

Sona directly uski taraf dekhti hai.

"Didi."

"Entire human history iss moment ka wait kar rahi thi."

Agent A cold tone me boli,

"Human history already enough people lose kar chuki hai."

Tension dangerous level par pahunch chuki thi.

Rohit tunnel ko stare karta raha.

Usko weird feeling aa rahi thi.

Fear bhi. Connection bhi.

Jaise woh jagah somehow usse recognize kar rahi ho.

Agent M quietly uske side me aaya.

"Bhai honestly."

"Mujhe ye bilkul pasand nahi aa raha."

"Same."

Suddenly tunnel ke andar se faint light visible hui.

Aur phir...

footsteps.

Slow. Heavy. Metallic.

Poora chamber silent ho gaya.

Scientists peeche hatne lage. Guards weapons ready karne lage.

Footsteps closer aati gayi.

Aur darkness me ek figure visible hua.

Old damaged astronaut suit. Broken helmet lights. Dust covered armor.

Agent A ka breathing stop jaisa ho gaya.

Figure slowly light me aaya.

Aur uske suit par faded name visible hua.

CAPTAIN ARYAN

Rohit whisper karta hai,

"Oh no."

Agent A ki aankhon me disbelief tha.

"Aryan."

Figure slowly head raise karta hai.

Lekin uski eyes completely black thi.

Aur distorted mechanical voice chamber me echo hui.

"THE GATE MUST OPEN."
