Chapter 5: The Void

Entire chamber reality distortion se crack ho raha tha.

Black fractures hawa me float kar rahe the.

Jaise glass toot raha ho... lekin space ka.

Scientists panic me readings check kar rahe the.

"Spatial collapse accelerating."

"Gate stability critical."

The Emperor still portal ke saamne khada tha.

Gigantic cosmic energy uske armor ke around flow kar rahi thi.

Aur uski white glowing eyes directly Rohit par locked thi.

Rohit abhi bhi shock me tha.

Velkari invaders nahi the.

Woh refugees the.

Aur universe me unse bhi dangerous cheez exist karti thi.

The Void.

Agent M slowly whisper karta hai,

"Mujhe Earth ka normal boring life suddenly bohot achha lag raha hai."

Even iss situation me Rohit halka sa smile kar deta hai.

Phir immediately serious ho jata hai.

"The Void exactly hai kya."

The Emperor slowly hand raise karta hai.

Entire chamber me new vision appear hoti hai.

Infinite darkness. No stars. No planets. No light.

Bas endless black void.

Aur woh darkness galaxies ko consume karti ja rahi thi.

Entire civilizations disappear ho rahi thi.

Without sound. Without explosion.

Jaise existence erase ho raha ho.

Sona horrified whisper karti hai,

"It's eating reality."

The Emperor nod karta hai.

"THE VOID CANNOT BE STOPPED."

"ONLY ESCAPED."

Agent A cold tone me boli,

"So you wanted Earth."

The Emperor directly answer deta hai,

"EARTH CONTAINS THE FINAL STABLE GATE."

Rohit slowly realize karta hai.

"That's why my bloodline exists."

The Emperor calmly bolta hai,

"YOUR ANCESTORS CREATED THE KEYS."

"ONLY YOUR DNA CAN CONTROL THE GATES."

Suddenly Rohit ke dimaag me strange flashes aane lagte hain.

Ancient humans. Advanced civilizations. Massive starships.

Aur ek ancient human empire spread across galaxies.

Rohit pain me head pakad leta hai.

"Aah."

Agent A instantly uske paas aati hai.

"Kya hua."

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"I can see them."

"Our ancestors."

Sona shock me uski taraf dekhti hai.

"Memory transfer."

Suddenly gate violently pulse karta hai.

Entire chamber tremble karta hai.

Portal ke andar se horrifying scream echo hoti hai.

Not Velkari.

Something else.

The Emperor instantly tense ho jata hai.

Even uski species fear feel kar sakti thi.

One Velkari warrior terrified telepathically scream karta hai,

"THE VOID HAS FOUND US."

Next second...

portal ke andar darkness move karti hai.

Not normal darkness. Alive darkness.

Poora chamber ka temperature suddenly drop ho jata hai.

Lights one by one off hone lagte hain.

Aur portal ke andar... two giant red eyes open hote hain.

Everyone freeze.

Even The Emperor peeche step leta hai.

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"What the hell is that."

No one answered.

Because nobody knew.

Suddenly one Velkari creature darkness ki taraf pull ho jata hai.

Aur instantly disappear.

No scream. No body.

Bas gone.

Entire chamber panic me aa gaya.

Scientists bhaagne lage. Velkari warriors weapons activate karne lage.

The Emperor loudly roar karta hai,

"CLOSE THE GATE."

Rohit shocked uski taraf dekhta hai.

"Tumhe toh gate open chahiye tha."

The Emperor directly answer deta hai,

"NOT FOR THEM."

Portal aur unstable hone lagta hai.

Black cracks poore cavern me spread hone lagte hain.

Aur slowly... dark shadow portal se bahar crawl karna start karti hai.

Reality literally uske touch se decay ho rahi thi.

Agent M quietly whisper karta hai,

"Okay."

"Now THIS is the final boss."
