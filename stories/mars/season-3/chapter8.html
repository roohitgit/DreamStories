Chapter 8: The Last Gatekeeper

Entire chamber shock me freeze ho gaya.

Captain Aryan darkness se slowly walk karte hue light me aaya.

Aur iss baar...

woh corrupted nahi lag raha tha.

Normal human. Broken. Injured. Exhausted.

Lekin alive.

Agent A ki aankhon me disbelief tha.

"Aryan."

Twenty years ka pain ek second me uske face par visible ho gaya.

Captain Aryan softly smile karta hai.

"Hi A."

Aur next second... Agent A directly uske paas bhaag kar usse hug kar leti hai.

Poora chamber silent tha.

Even Agent M emotional lag raha tha.

Rohit quietly whisper karta hai,

"Finally."

"Ek happy moment."

Sona ki eyes bhi slightly wet thi.

Aryan slowly Agent A ko dekhkar bolta hai,

"I tried to come back."

Agent A weak voice me poochti hai,

"Twenty years kaha the tum"

Aryan slowly The Emperor ki taraf dekhta hai.

"He saved me."

Everyone shocked.

The Emperor silently khada tha.

No arrogance. No anger.

Sirf exhaustion.

Aryan continue karta hai,

"The Void destroyed their worlds."

"I saw entire galaxies die."

Chamber completely silent ho gaya.

Aryan slowly Rohit ki taraf walk karta hai.

"And now everything depends on you."

Rohit awkward nervous smile deta hai.

"Again."

Aryan ancient control platform ki taraf point karta hai.

"The Gatekeepers created one final protocol."

AI projection instantly activate hoti hai.

FINAL PROTOCOL AVAILABLE LAST GATEKEEPER REQUIRED

Rohit confused poochta hai,

"Last Gatekeeper"

AI calmly answer karti hai,

"The final living descendant."

"Rohit Badole."

Suddenly holographic platform fully activate ho jata hai.

Entire galaxy map chamber me expand ho jata hai.

Thousands gates visible hote hain.

Aur har gate rapidly destabilize ho raha tha.

AI softly explain karti hai,

"You can permanently seal the network."

"Or fully reopen it."

Sona immediately poochti hai,

"What happens if he reopens it"

AI answer deti hai,

"The Velkari survive."

"But the Void gains access to every connected galaxy."

Agent A quietly poochti hai,

"And sealing it"

AI pause karti hai.

Then softly answer karti hai,

"All trapped civilizations will eventually die."

Poora room silent.

No correct choice existed.

Suddenly chamber violently shake hota hai.

Darkness rapidly entrance se andar spread hone lagti hai.

The Void was almost here.

The Emperor instantly defensive stance me aa jata hai.

Velkari warriors weapons activate karte hain.

Aur first time... The Emperor directly Rohit ke saamne kneel karta hai.

"Please."

Poora room freeze ho gaya.

Ancient cosmic ruler... begging.

"We only want to live."

Rohit speechless tha.

Usko suddenly sab samajh aane laga.

Velkari monsters nahi the.

Humans heroes nahi the.

Everyone bas survive karna chahta tha.

Suddenly darkness chamber me enter karti hai.

Aur air instantly decay hone lagti hai.

The Void finally aa chuka tha.

Do giant red eyes darkness me open hote hain.

Reality walls dissolve hone lagti hain.

AI warning scream karti hai,

"FINAL COLLAPSE IMMINENT."

Rohit slowly control platform ke center me walk karta hai.

Everyone uski taraf dekh raha tha.

Entire galaxies ka future... ab uske decision par depend karta tha.

Aur platform suddenly poochta hai.

SEAL THE GATES OR REOPEN THE NETWORK

Rohit slowly aankhen band karta hai.

Aur finally... decision lene wala tha.
