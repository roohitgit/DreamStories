Chapter 1: The War Begins

Mars sky fire se bhar chuka tha.

Gigantic Velkari ships red clouds ke upar silently hover kar rahe the.

Colony Seven ke defense cannons nonstop plasma blasts fire kar rahe the.

Explosions poore atmosphere me visible the.

Aur har direction me panic.

Humans finally realize kar chuke the.

They were not alone in the universe.

Aur jo species saamne thi...

woh friendly bilkul nahi thi.

Rohit command center ke middle me khada heavily breathing kar raha tha.

Uske chest par glowing alien symbol abhi bhi visible tha.

Sona continuously battle reports receive kar rahi thi.

"North shield sector collapse."

"Mining dome destroyed."

"Casualties increasing."

Agent M quietly whisper karta hai,

"Great."

"Vacation officially ruined."

Even iss chaos me Rohit halka sa hass diya.

Stress level already beyond human limit tha.

Suddenly giant explosion colony ke outer sector me hota hai.

Entire command center shake ho jata hai.

One officer panic me chillata hai,

"Velkari walkers breached the eastern wall."

Main hologram screen par giant alien machines visible hue.

Tower size black armored creatures. Glowing white cores. Heavy energy weapons.

Rohit speechless ho gaya.

"Bhai."

"Unke paas transformers bhi hain."

Agent A immediately tactical map activate karti hai.

"We can't hold them for long."

Sona serious tone me boli,

"Then we stop the gate."

Room instantly silent ho gaya.

Sabko pata tha.

Gate hi source tha.

Agar gate band hua... shayad invasion ruk sakta tha.

Rohit slowly poochta hai,

"Aur agar nahi hua"

Nobody answered.

Aur wahi answer tha.

Suddenly entire room lights dim ho gayi.

Rohit ka glowing symbol brighter hone laga.

Voice phir uske dimaag me echo hui.

"RETURN TO THE GATE."

Rohit pain me head pakad leta hai.

"Aah."

Agent A immediately uske paas aayi.

"They're connecting to him again."

Sona quickly scans run karti hai.

Holographic DNA patterns screen par appear hote hain.

Aur suddenly...

one hidden file unlock hoti hai.

CLASSIFIED RECORD PROJECT KEYSTONE

Everyone freeze ho gaya.

Agent A shocked whisper karti hai,

"No."

Sona file open karti hai.

Old Earth government logs visible hote hain.

Ancient Mars expedition footage. Secret experiments. Genetic modifications.

Aur ek name repeatedly visible tha.

ROHIT BADOLE BLOODLINE

Rohit literally blink karta reh gaya.

"Mera family tree NASA project kyun lag raha hai."

Sona quietly explain karti hai,

"Your ancestors were connected to the first gate discovery."

"Generations ago."

Agent M confused expression me bola,

"Matlab aliens ne family subscription le rakhi hai kya."

Suddenly screen par ancient video play hota hai.

Old scientist camera ke saamne terrified state me bol raha tha.

"The gate reacts only to compatible DNA."

"We created a bloodline capable of controlling it."

"One day the Key will return to Mars."

Video abruptly cut ho jata hai.

Poora room silent.

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"So my whole life..."

"Was planned."

Agent A softly uski taraf dekhti hai.

"Not by us."

Suddenly alarms aur loud ho jaate hain.

Officer panic me scream karta hai,

"Velkari entering central sector."

Massive explosions colony center ke closer visible hone lagte hain.

Time khatam ho raha tha.

Sona directly Rohit ki taraf dekhti hai.

"Only you can close the gate."

Rohit nervous smile deta hai.

"No pressure."

Agent A weapon load karti hai.

"Then let's finish this."

Aur poori team once again underground gate ki taraf move karne lagi...

jabki Mars ke upar...

Humanity ki first galactic war rage kar rahi thi.
