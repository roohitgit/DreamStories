Chapter 7: The Gatekeepers

Ancient chamber complete silence me doob gaya.

Sirf distant explosions aur collapsing tunnels ki awaaz sunayi de rahi thi.

AI projection still Rohit ke saamne calmly floating thi.

"You must close every gate forever."

Ye sentence sabke dimaag me echo ho raha tha.

Sona slowly peeche hati.

"If we close the gates..."

"The Velkari are doomed."

AI immediately answer karti hai,

"If the gates remain open..."

"The Void consumes all realities."

Poora room silent ho gaya.

No good option existed anymore.

Agent M quietly whisper karta hai,

"Classic humanity."

"Always trapped between terrible and worse."

Rohit slowly ancient control device ki taraf walk karta hai.

Device ke surface par same glowing symbol visible tha.

Jaise woh uska wait kar raha ho.

Agent A softly poochti hai,

"Can you actually control it"

Rohit nervous expression me bola,

"Maine life me TV remote properly control nahi kiya."

Even iss tension me Agent M halka sa hass diya.

Suddenly chamber violently shake hota hai.

BOOOOM.

One wall partially collapse ho jaati hai.

Aur darkness slowly andar spread hone lagti hai.

The Void closer aa raha tha.

AI urgently bolti hai,

"The Emperor cannot hold it much longer."

Sona shock me poochti hai,

"He's fighting alone"

AI nod karti hai.

"The Velkari once protected entire galaxies from the Void."

"Now they are the last survivors."

Suddenly Rohit ke dimaag me new visions flash hote hain.

Ancient human fleets. Velkari armies. Massive cosmic war.

Aur center me... Gatekeepers.

Humans wearing glowing armor. Controlling giant gates across galaxies.

Rohit pain me head pakad leta hai.

"Aah."

AI directly uski taraf dekhti hai.

"The memories are awakening."

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"We abandoned them."

AI quietly answer deti hai,

"The Gatekeepers sealed the network to save existence."

"Billions were sacrificed."

Agent A softly poochti hai,

"So humanity ran away."

AI silent ho jaati hai.

Aur wahi answer tha.

Suddenly chamber entrance par giant explosion hota hai.

Everyone instantly turn karta hai.

The Emperor heavily damaged state me andar enter karta hai.

Black armor cracked. White energy unstable.

Aur uske behind... darkness spread ho rahi thi.

The Void was coming.

The Emperor directly Rohit ke saamne kneel karta hai.

Entire room freeze.

Ancient cosmic ruler... kneeling before a normal human.

"The Heir must decide."

Rohit stunned tha.

"Tum mujhe decision dena chahte ho"

The Emperor slowly nod karta hai.

"Close the gates."

"And doom us."

"Or reopen the network."

"And risk the Void spreading."

Agent M quietly bola,

"Fantastic."

"No pressure at all."

Suddenly dark tendrils chamber ke entrance se andar crawl karne lagte hain.

Reality walls dissolve hone lagti hain.

Scientists instantly erased ho jaate hain.

No bodies. No screams.

Bas nothing.

Agent A immediately weapon ready karti hai.

"Decision now."

Rohit slowly ancient control platform ke center me walk karta hai.

Jaise hi woh platform touch karta hai... entire chamber activate ho jata hai.

Gigantic holographic galaxy map poore room me appear hota hai.

Thousands gates visible hote hain. Across stars. Across galaxies.

Aur sab slowly collapse ho rahe the.

AI softly bolti hai,

"The network is dying."

Rohit center me khada deep breath leta hai.

Uski eyes me fear tha. Pressure tha. Responsibility thi.

Aur phir...

ek new voice chamber me echo hoti hai.

Human voice.

"Rohit."

Everyone instantly turn karta hai.

Dark tunnel ke andar se... Captain Aryan slowly walk kar raha tha.

Alive.
