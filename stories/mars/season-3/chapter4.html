Chapter 4: The Emperor

Entire gate chamber violently shake ho raha tha.

Portal unstable energy se explode jaisa lag raha tha. Black lightning har direction me strike kar rahi thi.

Aur portal ke andar...

that massive presence slowly closer aa rahi thi.

Even Velkari warrior fear me peeche hat gaya.

Rohit confused tha.

"Wait."

"Tum log bhi kisi se darte ho."

Alien warrior directly portal ko stare karte hue whisper karta hai,

"THE EMPEROR DESTROYS EVERYTHING."

Suddenly entire chamber me unbearable pressure feel hone laga.

Jaise gravity increase ho gayi ho.

Scientists aur guards literally ground par girne lage.

Agent M struggle karte hue bola,

"Mujhe lag raha hai gym ka revenge chal raha hai."

Even Sona shock me portal ko dekh rahi thi.

Portal ke andar giant glowing eyes visible hue.

Huge. Ancient. Terrifying.

Aur phir...

a gigantic black hand portal ke edge par place hota hai.

BOOOOOM.

Entire cavern crack ho jata hai.

Rohit instinctively peeche hatta hai.

"Nahi bhai."

"Ye toh Avengers final boss level hai."

The Emperor slowly portal se bahar emerge karna start karta hai.

Massive armored body stars jaisa black. White burning eyes. Floating cosmic energy.

Aur uske around reality distort ho rahi thi.

Jaise universe itself usko reject kar raha ho.

Every Velkari creature instantly kneel kar gaya.

Complete silence.

Then... The Emperor directly Rohit ki taraf dekhta hai.

Aur telepathic voice poore Mars me echo hoti hai.

"THE HEIR RETURNS."

Entire colony lights flicker karne lagti hain.

Mars orbit me ships simultaneously activate ho jaate hain.

Earth satellites signal lose kar dete hain.

Poora solar system suddenly silent feel hone lagta hai.

Rohit nervous whisper karta hai,

"Mujhe ye attention bilkul pasand nahi aa raha."

The Emperor slowly hand raise karta hai.

Aur Rohit ke chest ka glowing symbol brighter ho jata hai.

Unke beech invisible connection visible tha.

Agent A immediately Rohit ke saamne khadi ho gayi.

"Stay away from him."

The Emperor uski taraf dekhta bhi nahi.

Jaise humans uske liye insignificant ho.

Sona slowly whisper karti hai,

"He's ancient."

"Maybe older than humanity itself."

Suddenly The Emperor ke around floating holographic symbols appear hote hain.

Entire galaxy maps. Destroyed civilizations. Dead planets.

Aur center me... Earth.

The Emperor telepathically bolta hai,

"THE FINAL GATE MUST OPEN."

Rohit finally directly poochta hai,

"WHY."

For few seconds complete silence.

Then The Emperor answer deta hai.

"TO SAVE REALITY."

Everyone freeze ho gaya.

Agent M blink karta hai.

"Excuse me what."

The Emperor hand wave karta hai.

Suddenly giant cosmic vision poore chamber me appear hoti hai.

Galaxies collapsing. Stars disappearing. Black void universe ko consume karta hua.

Aur thousands civilizations running from it.

Rohit horrified whisper karta hai,

"What is that."

The Emperor slowly answer deta hai.

"THE VOID."

"THE END OF ALL EXISTENCE."

Sona speechless thi.

Agent A slowly realize karti hai.

"You're not invading."

The Emperor directly uski taraf dekhta hai.

"WE ARE ESCAPING."

Poora chamber dead silent.

Suddenly sab kuch change ho gaya.

Velkari monsters nahi the.

They were refugees.

Aur Earth...

next target nahi.

Next shelter thi.

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"So all this war..."

The Emperor interrupt karta hai.

"YOUR ANCESTORS CLOSED THE GATES."

"THEY ABANDONED US."

Portal violently unstable hone lagta hai.

The Emperor suddenly pain me roar karta hai.

Reality distort hone lagti hai.

Aur giant black cracks air me appear hone lagte hain.

Scientist terrified scream karta hai,

"Space-time collapse."

The gate was breaking.

Aur agar woh collapse hua...

shayad poora Mars destroy ho sakta tha.
