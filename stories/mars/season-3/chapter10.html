Chapter 10: A New Universe

DIMENSIONAL REDIRECTION STARTED

AI ki voice poore chamber me echo ho rahi thi.

Entire gate network galaxy map par activate hone laga.

Thousands glowing pathways stars ke beech illuminate ho gaye.

Aur center me... The Void violently react kar raha tha.

Darkness poore chamber me explode ho gayi.

Reality collapse hone lagi. Mars surface tremble karne laga.

Aur giant red eyes directly Rohit par locked thi.

"YOU CANNOT ESCAPE THE END."

Rohit control platform firmly hold karta hai.

Uska chest symbol brightest glow me shine kar raha tha.

AI rapidly instructions announce karti hai,

"Network synchronization unstable."

"Manual control required."

Rohit panic me bola,

"Manual matlab kya."

AI calmly answer karti hai,

"You must guide the entire gate network yourself."

Agent M instantly react karta hai,

"Of course."

"Why would universal crisis ever be simple."

Suddenly darkness giant wave ki tarah chamber ki taraf rush karti hai.

The Emperor immediately cosmic shield create karta hai.

BOOOOOOM.

Massive collision entire chamber shake kar deta hai.

The Emperor struggle kar raha tha.

Even uski ancient power enough nahi thi.

Captain Aryan loudly scream karta hai,

"Rohit now."

Rohit eyes close karta hai.

Aur suddenly...

uske around complete galaxy network visible hone lagta hai.

Not hologram.

Real.

Infinite stars. Infinite gates. Infinite worlds.

He could feel everything.

Human colonies. Velkari survivors. Entire civilizations waiting.

Aur peeche... The Void approaching.

Rohit deep breath leta hai.

"Okay."

"Let's do this."

Uske hands glowing energy se surround hone lagte hain.

Aur one by one... gate pathways redirect hone lagte hain.

Entire network slowly Null Dimension ki taraf shift hone laga.

The Void violently roar karta hai.

Reality storms every direction me explode hone lagte hain.

AI warning scream karti hai,

"Energy overload."

"User life signs unstable."

Agent A instantly panic me Rohit ki taraf dekhti hai.

"What's happening."

AI softly answer karti hai,

"The Gatekeeper is merging with the network."

Poora room silent ho gaya.

Sona slowly whisper karti hai,

"He won't survive."

Rohit unki taraf dekhta hai.

Aur first time... woh completely calm lag raha tha.

"Honestly."

"Mars trip ka ending kaafi extreme ho gaya."

Even Agent A emotional smile kar deti hai.

The Emperor directly Rohit ke saamne kneel karta hai.

Ancient ruler respectfully bow karta hua.

"You saved us."

Rohit softly answer deta hai,

"Save everyone."

Suddenly giant light burst poore chamber me spread hoti hai.

Entire gate network fully redirect ho jata hai.

Aur The Void... slowly Null Dimension ki taraf pull hone lagta hai.

Darkness violently resist karti hai. Red eyes rage me glow karte hain.

Lekin thousands gates simultaneously activate hote hain.

Aur finally... The Void completely seal ho jata hai.

Complete silence.

Black cracks disappear ho jaate hain. Reality stabilize hone lagti hai. Mars tremors stop ho jaate hain.

War... finally khatam ho chuki thi.

Slowly light fade hoti hai.

Aur everyone shock me chamber ko dekh raha tha.

The Void gone. The gates stable. Universe safe.

Lekin...

Rohit missing tha.

Agent A instantly panic me idhar udhar dekhti hai.

"Rohit."

No response.

Sona quietly whisper karti hai,

"He merged with the network."

Agent M silent khada tha.

For the first time... no jokes.

Suddenly ancient platform softly glow karta hai.

Aur ek final holographic message appear hota hai.

FROM: ROHIT

Message automatically play hota hai.

Rohit smiling face screen par visible hota hai.

"Hello losers."

Agent M instantly emotional laugh karta hai.

Rohit continue karta hai,

"Agar tum ye dekh rahe ho..."

"Matlab main officially cosmic WiFi ban chuka hoon."

Even Sona smile kar deti hai.

Rohit softly bolta hai,

"Protect Earth."

"Protect the gates."

"And Agent M..."

"Chai tapri zarur kholna."

Agent M emotional tone me bola,

"Pakki baat."

Final message slowly fade hota hai.

Aur chamber ke ceiling se first time sunlight visible hoti hai.

Mars sky clear ho chuka tha.

Humanity survived. Velkari survived. The universe survived.

Aur somewhere beyond stars...

Rohit still watched over the gates.

MARS KE RAAZ THE END
