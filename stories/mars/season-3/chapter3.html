Chapter 2: Return To The Gate

Colony Seven officially collapse ke edge par thi.

Sky me Velkari ships continuously descend kar rahe the. Defense towers explode ho rahe the. Aur emergency sirens nonstop echo kar rahe the.

Lekin Rohit aur uski team phir se underground gate ki taraf ja rahe the.

Exactly wahi jagah... jahan se ye nightmare start hua tha.

Emergency military transport dark tunnels me rapidly move kar raha tha.

Agent M vehicle drive kar raha tha.

Aur surprisingly... still jokes maar raha tha.

"Agar hum survive hue na."

"Main Earth par chai tapri kholunga."

Rohit side seat par bola,

"Main free me aaunga."

"Tu paise dega."

"Alien invasion me bhi business minded."

Even Agent A ke face par halka smile aa gaya.

Few seconds ke liye sab normal feel hua.

Phir suddenly tunnel violently shake hua.

BOOOOM.

Vehicle almost flip hone wala tha.

Sona quickly hologram screen check karti hai.

"Velkari underground bhi aa gaye."

Poora atmosphere instantly serious ho gaya.

Rohit quietly poochta hai,

"Tumhe sach me lagta hai gate close ho sakta hai"

Sona directly uski taraf dekhti hai.

"I don't know."

First time... Mars ki powerful ruler uncertain lag rahi thi.

Agent A softly boli,

"But we have to try."

Vehicle deeper underground enter karta hai.

Tunnel walls par strange alien symbols visible hone lagte hain.

Glowing red markings. Unknown language.

Aur jaise jaise Rohit unke paas se pass hota... symbols brighter glow karte.

Agent M nervous tone me bola,

"Bhai."

"Tu definitely haunted hai."

Rohit irritate hokar bola,

"Mujhe bhi ab doubt aa raha hai."

Suddenly vehicle systems glitch hone lagte hain.

Lights flicker. Screens distort.

Aur same telepathic voice phir echo hoti hai.

"THE KEY RETURNS."

Rohit ka glowing chest symbol aur brighter ho gaya.

Agent A instantly worried ho gayi.

"Connection stronger ho raha hai."

Sona quietly observe kar rahi thi.

Jaise woh simultaneously scared bhi ho... aur fascinated bhi.

Finally vehicle stop hua.

Gate chamber entrance unke saamne tha.

Lekin ab woh pehle jaisa nahi lag raha tha.

Entire cavern black alien growth se covered tha.

Walls pulse kar rahi thi. Floor move karta hua feel ho raha tha.

Jaise poori jagah alive ho.

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"Nahi bhai."

"Ye toh proper horror game ban gaya."

Team cautiously andar enter karti hai.

Aur center me... The Gate still active tha.

Massive swirling portal stars aur darkness se filled.

Aur iss baar... portal ke around multiple Velkari creatures kneel position me khade the.

Jaise kisi god ko worship kar rahe ho.

One creature slowly head raise karta hai.

White glowing eyes directly Rohit par lock ho jaati hain.

Phir telepathic voice chamber me echo hoti hai.

"THE CREATOR RETURNS."

Poora group freeze ho gaya.

Rohit confused panic me bola,

"EXCUSE ME WHAT."

Suddenly all Velkari creatures simultaneously kneel karte hain.

Sona shocked whisper karti hai,

"They're bowing to him."

Rohit literally peeche hata.

"Nahi."

"Mujhe ye bilkul pasand nahi aa raha."

Portal ke andar suddenly giant silhouette visible hota hai.

Way bigger than the others.

Massive armored figure slowly darkness me move karta hua.

Entire chamber tremble karne lagta hai.

Aur telepathic voice once again echo hoti hai.

"OPEN THE FINAL DOOR."
