Chapter 9: The Choice

Entire ancient chamber darkness aur collapsing reality ke beech shake ho raha tha.

Black cracks air me spread ho rahe the. Walls dissolve ho rahi thi.

Aur center me...

Rohit control platform par khada tha.

SEAL THE GATES OR REOPEN THE NETWORK

Ye words continuously glow kar rahe the.

The Void chamber ke andar slowly spread ho raha tha.

Do giant red eyes darkness me stare kar rahe the.

Aur every second... reality weaker hoti ja rahi thi.

The Emperor still kneeling position me tha.

Ancient cosmic ruler. Powerful beyond imagination.

Lekin iss moment me... sirf desperate.

"We only want to survive."

Rohit uski taraf dekhta hai.

Phir Agent A ki taraf.

Phir Sona.

Phir Earth ka hologram.

Billions lives. Entire civilizations. Complete galaxies.

Sab uske decision par depend kar raha tha.

Agent M quietly uske side me aata hai.

"Bhai."

"Jo bhi kar."

"Fast kar."

Even iss situation me Rohit halka sa hass deta hai.

Phir slowly serious ho jata hai.

Rohit softly poochta hai,

"Agar main gates seal kar du..."

The Emperor eyes close karta hai.

"The Velkari civilization ends."

"And millions trapped beyond the network die."

Rohit ka heart sink ho gaya.

"Aur reopen"

AI immediately answer karti hai,

"The Void gains full access to this universe."

Agent A quietly whisper karti hai,

"So either way..."

"People die."

Poora room silent.

Suddenly darkness rapidly chamber ke closer aati hai.

One Velkari warrior instantly consume ho jata hai.

Gone.

As if he never existed.

The Emperor loudly roar karta hai.

Aur white cosmic energy blast darkness ko temporarily push back karti hai.

Lekin woh weak ho raha tha.

Very weak.

Captain Aryan slowly Rohit ke paas aata hai.

"You know what real Gatekeepers did"

Rohit quietly poochta hai,

"What"

Aryan softly smile karta hai.

"They found another way."

Everyone instantly uski taraf mudta hai.

Sona shocked tone me poochti hai,

"Another way exists"

Aryan ancient platform ki taraf point karta hai.

"The gates don't just connect places."

"They connect dimensions."

AI suddenly realize karti hai.

"Impossible."

Aryan directly Rohit ki taraf dekhta hai.

"You can redirect the network."

Rohit confused.

"Matlab"

Aryan deep breath leta hai.

"Trap the Void somewhere else."

Entire chamber silent ho gaya.

AI immediately warning tone me bolti hai,

"That protocol was abandoned."

"Success probability less than one percent."

Agent M instantly bola,

"Fantastic."

"Our best plan is cosmic gambling."

Suddenly chamber collapse aur faster hone lagta hai.

Time khatam ho raha tha.

The Void roar karta hai.

Entire Mars surface shake hoti hai.

Rohit finally deep breath leta hai.

Aur control platform par hand place karta hai.

Instantly thousands holographic star systems uske around appear hote hain.

Infinite gates. Infinite worlds. Infinite possibilities.

Aur center me... one dark empty dimension visible thi.

Completely isolated.

AI quietly whisper karti hai,

"The Null Dimension."

"No life."

"No stars."

"Nothing."

Rohit slowly smile karta hai.

"Perfect jail."

The Emperor shocked expression me uski taraf dekhta hai.

"You would save us"

Rohit directly answer deta hai,

"I won't let entire civilizations die."

Agent A proud expression me usko dekh rahi thi.

Sona softly smile karti hai.

Even The Emperor first time respectful bow karta hai.

Rohit control symbols activate karta hai.

Entire gate network suddenly glow hone lagta hai.

Aur AI loudly announce karti hai,

DIMENSIONAL REDIRECTION STARTED

Suddenly The Void violently react karta hai.

Darkness poore chamber me explode ho jaati hai.

Aur giant red eyes directly Rohit par lock ho jaate hain.

"YOU CANNOT ESCAPE THE END."
