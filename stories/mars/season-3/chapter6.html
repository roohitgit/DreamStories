Chapter 6: The Darkness Beyond

Dark shadow slowly portal se bahar crawl kar rahi thi.

Aur jahan woh touch karti... reality distort hone lagti.

Metal walls black dust me convert ho rahe the. Lights instantly disappear ho rahe the.

Jaise existence itself erase ho raha ho.

Entire chamber terror me freeze ho gaya.

Even Velkari warriors fear me peeche hat rahe the.

The Emperor loudly roar karta hai,

"DO NOT LET IT CROSS."

Suddenly dozens Velkari warriors simultaneously energy weapons fire karte hain.

Massive white plasma blasts darkness ko hit karte hain.

Lekin... nothing happens.

Energy attacks simply disappear ho jaate hain.

Agent M slowly whisper karta hai,

"Great."

"Immortal darkness monster."

"Exactly what we needed."

Dark shadow aur bahar aati hai.

Ab uska shape visible tha.

Humanoid. But incomplete.

Floating black smoke jaisa body. Giant red glowing eyes.

Aur uske around air literally decay ho rahi thi.

Rohit chest pain feel karta hai.

Uska glowing symbol violently react kar raha tha.

Voice suddenly uske dimaag me echo hoti hai.

"THE LAST KEY."

Rohit instantly scream karta hai.

"Aahhh."

Dark entity directly uski taraf turn karti hai.

Entire chamber temperature aur drop ho jata hai.

Sona horrified whisper karti hai,

"It's aware of him."

The Emperor immediately Rohit ke saamne aa jata hai.

Massive cosmic shield activate hota hai.

"YOU CANNOT HAVE THE HEIR."

Dark entity first time speak karti hai.

Voice human nahi thi.

Jaise thousands broken voices ek saath bol rahi ho.

"ALL THINGS END."

Entire cavern shake ho jata hai.

Black shockwave every direction me spread hoti hai.

Scientists instantly disappear ho jaate hain.

Not dead.

Gone.

Reality se erased.

Agent A immediately Rohit ko pull karti hai.

"Move."

Poora team rapidly chamber ke side tunnels ki taraf run karta hai.

Behind them... The Emperor aur dark entity impossible battle fight kar rahe the.

White cosmic energy versus living darkness.

Each impact se reality crack ho rahi thi.

Mars surface tak tremors feel ho rahe the.

Rohit running while panic me bola,

"Mujhe normal office job karni chahiye thi."

Agent M bhi bhaagte hue bola,

"Abhi bhi kar le."

Suddenly tunnel ke walls par ancient symbols glow hone lagte hain.

Aur hidden door open hota hai.

Inside... an ancient chamber.

Different from Velkari technology.

Human architecture.

Advanced human architecture.

Sona shocked whisper karti hai,

"This place was built by ancient humans."

Center me giant circular device placed tha.

Aur uske middle me same symbol visible tha jo Rohit ke chest par glow kar raha tha.

Agent A slowly realize karti hai.

"This is a control chamber."

Suddenly ancient systems automatically activate ho jaate hain.

Holographic AI appear hoti hai.

Human female projection. White glowing eyes. Silver suit.

Aur woh directly Rohit ko dekhkar bolti hai,

"Welcome back, Commander."

Poora room silent.

Rohit blink karta reh gaya.

"Commander"

AI calmly answer karti hai,

"You are the final descendant of the Gatekeepers."

Sona whisper karti hai,

"The rulers of the gates."

AI nod karti hai.

"Humanity once ruled thousands of worlds."

"Until the Void arrived."

Suddenly behind them giant explosion hota hai.

Tunnel collapse hone lagta hai.

Aur darkness rapidly chamber ki taraf spread kar rahi thi.

AI immediately urgent tone me bolti hai,

"If the Void enters this universe completely..."

"Everything ends."

Rohit slowly poochta hai,

"Then how do we stop it"

AI directly uski eyes me dekhti hai.

Aur softly answer deti hai,

"You must close every gate forever."

Poora room silent ho gaya.

Because everyone knew.

Agar gates close hue...

Velkari civilization permanently trapped ho jayegi.
