Chapter 5: Exam Center Ki Raat

Special Mars examination event finally aa gaya.

Aur Rohit poore mission mode me tha.

Black hoodie. Cap. Backpack.

Sameer usko dekhkar shocked tha.

"Bhai tu exam dene ja raha hai ya bank lootne."

Rohit serious tone me bola,

"Aaj sach pata chalega."

Night time me futuristic exam center aur bhi dangerous lag raha tha.

Blue neon lights. Flying drones. Armed robot guards.

Aur giant Mars hologram sky me rotate kar raha tha.

Sameer video call par whisper karta hai,

"Mujhe already darr lag raha hai."

Rohit quietly side gate ki taraf badha.

Usne ek service entrance spot kiya.

Door partially open tha.

"Too easy."

Woh andar ghus gaya.

Andar poora area silent tha.

Long metallic corridors. Dim lights. Security cameras.

Har jagah futuristic vibe.

Ek cleaning robot suddenly uske saamne aa gaya.

"Please maintain cleanliness."

Rohit almost heart attack le leta.

"Bhai tu chupke se mat aaya kar."

Robot calmly bola,

"Confusion detected."

Rohit side se nikal gaya.

Control room ke paas security guard khada tha.

Guard ne Rohit ko dekha.

"Kaun ho tum"

Rohit instantly confident mode me aa gaya.

"Candidate."

Guard confused.

"Exam kal hai."

Rohit bina blink kiye bola,

"Main early preparation karta hoon."

Guard do second usko stare karta raha.

Phir surprisingly side hat gaya.

Sameer video call par literally scream kar raha tha.

"YE WORK KAISE KAR GAYA."

Rohit proud tone me bola,

"Confidence."

Woh deeper area me enter hua.

Suddenly ek giant hall saamne aaya.

Inside multiple scientists hologram screens par kaam kar rahe the.

Mars maps. Alien symbols. Navigation data.

Rohit quietly whisper karta hai,

"Ye exam center kam secret agency zyada lag raha hai."

Uski nazar ek isolated glass chamber par gayi.

Andar ek woman quietly baithi thi.

Black coat. Sharp eyes. Calm expression.

Table par files aur holographic screens floating the.

Rohit freeze ho gaya.

"Agent A."

Sameer panic mode me tha.

"Bhai bhaag wahan se."

Lekin Rohit already room ki taraf badh chuka tha.

Door automatically open hua.

Agent A ne slowly nazar uthayi.

Poora room silent ho gaya.

Rohit ka gussa instantly bahar aa gaya.

"Aap mujhe har baar fail kyun karti ho"

Agent A quietly usko dekhte rahi.

Koi panic nahi. Koi surprise nahi.

Jaise woh already expect kar rahi thi.

Few seconds silence.

Phir woh softly muskuraayi.

Aur bas itna boli,

"Tum pass ho jaate ho."

Rohit blink karta raha.

"WHAT."

"Isliye fail karti hoon."

Rohit ka dimaag officially reboot mode me chala gaya.

"Ye kya logic hai."

Agent A chair se slightly lean hui.

"Mars safe nahi hai."

Rohit instantly bola,

"Phir colony kyun banayi"

Agent A ne sigh li.

"Kyuki humans stubborn hote hain."

Rohit do second chup raha.

Phir bola,

"Ye baat toh sahi hai."

Agent A first time halka sa hasi.

Rohit notice karta hai ki uski eyes me weird sadness thi.

Jaise woh kuch hide kar rahi ho.

Rohit slowly poochta hai,

"Aap mujhse kya chhupa rahi hain"

Agent A ka expression instantly serious ho gaya.

"Tumhe Mars nahi jaana chahiye."

"Par kyun"

Agent A answer dene hi wali thi...

Tabhi room ka alarm suddenly activate ho gaya.

WARNING UNAUTHORIZED ENTRY DETECTED

Red lights poore corridor me flash hone lagi.

Sameer literally chilla raha tha.

"BHAI MAINE PEHLE HI BOLA THA."

Security drones activate ho gaye.

Agent A instantly khadi hui.

"Great. Tumne pura system alert kar diya."

Rohit nervous smile deta hai.

"Technically main akela nahi tha. Sameer bhi call par tha."

"THANKS BHAI."

Agent A quickly ek hidden exit open karti hai.

"Idhar se niklo."

Rohit shocked.

"Aap meri help kar rahi hain"

Agent A uski taraf directly dekhti hai.

"Main tumhe bachane ki koshish kar rahi hoon."

Rohit us line ka meaning samajhne ki koshish karta raha.

Lekin Agent A already opposite direction me security ko distract karne chali gayi thi.

Aur Rohit pehli baar realize karta hai...

Shayad Agent A villain nahi thi.
