Chapter 1: Rohit Aur Uska Mars Ka Junoon

Saal 2095.

Duniya completely change ho chuki thi.

Flying taxis normal thi. AI teachers school me padha rahe the. Aur Mars par humans ki colony bhi ban chuki thi.

Lekin Rohit ka dimaag bachpan se sirf ek cheez par atka hua tha.

Mars.

Dusre bachche cricket cards collect karte the. Rohit Mars ke photos collect karta tha.

Uske room ki walls par sirf red planet ke posters lage hue the.

Ek baar uski mummy room me aayi aur boli,

"Beta ye normal room hai ya ISRO branch office?"

Rohit telescope clean karte hue bola,

"Mummy ek din Mars par mera ghar hoga."

Mummy ne seedha jawab diya,

"Pehle Earth par apna room clean kar."

School me bhi Rohit alag hi level ka banda tha.

Teacher ne poocha,

"Bade hoke kya banoge?"

Ek student bola doctor. Dusra engineer. Teesra businessman.

Rohit confidently bola,

"Main Mars explorer banunga."

Class me silence ho gaya.

Teacher ne chashma utaar kar poocha,

"Homework complete kiya?"

Rohit dheere se bola,

"Nahi."

Teacher boli,

"Mars cancel."

Pure class me hasi phoot padi.

Lekin Rohit serious tha.

Wo har raat terrace par jaake Mars dekhta tha. Kabhi kabhi itna stare karta tha ki pados wali aunty ko lagta tha Rohit spying kar raha hai.

Ek din aunty ne directly complaint kar di.

"Ye ladka roz hamari balcony ki taraf dekhta rehta hai."

Rohit shock ho gaya.

"Aunty main Mars dekh raha tha."

Aunty boli,

"Achha to Mars hamare building ke peeche hai kya?"

Rohit ka best friend Sameer bhi uska mazaak udaata rehta tha.

"Bhai tu Mars jaake karega kya?"

Rohit bola,

"Colony banaunga."

Sameer bola,

"WiFi ka password pehle mujhe bhejna."

Time ke saath Rohit ka obsession aur dangerous hota gaya.

Wo Mars Colony Seven ke baare me har news follow karta tha.

Aur ek naam baar baar saamne aata tha.

Sona.

Mars Colony Seven ki ruler.

Log usse Mars ki Maharani bolte the.

Kuch log kehte the wo genius hai. Kuch log kehte the wo dangerous hai.

Ek news channel par uska interview chal raha tha.

Reporter bola,

"Humanity ka future kya hai?"

Sona muskuraayi.

"Mars sirf beginning hai."

Rohit TV ke saamne freeze ho gaya.

Usne dheere se bola,

"One day... main bhi Mars jaunga."

Usse nahi pata tha... ki Mars already uska wait kar raha tha.
