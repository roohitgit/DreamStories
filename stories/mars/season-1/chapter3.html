Chapter 3: Har Baar Fail

Mars selection results ka din finally aa gaya.

Puri duniya ke candidates result portal refresh kar rahe the jaise stock market crash hone wala ho.

Aur Rohit subah se laptop ke saamne baitha tha.

Table par coffee. Chips. Chocolate. Aur stress. Sab ek saath pade the.

Uska dost Sameer video call par tha.

"Bhai calm reh."

Rohit refresh button dabaate hue bola,

"Server load le nahi paa raha matlab main top kiya hoon."

"Ya phir duniya bhar ke log bhi same kaam kar rahe hain."

Finally screen load hui.

RESULT AVAILABLE

Rohit ne deep breath li.

"Bhai agar iss baar pass ho gaya na toh terrace par dance karunga."

Sameer instantly bola,

"Please video mat daalna."

Rohit ne result open kiya.

Aur next five seconds tak woh chup raha.

Sameer nervous ho gaya.

"Kya hua"

Rohit screen ki taraf blank stare kar raha tha.

Physics 100 Navigation 99 Survival 98 Zero Gravity 100 Mental Simulation 97

Sameer chair se uchal gaya.

"BHAI TU TOH MARS KA TOPPER NIKLA."

Rohit ka face slowly smile me convert hone laga.

Phir uski nazar last line par gayi.

COGNITIVE STABILITY : FAIL FINAL STATUS : REJECTED

Room silent.

Sameer blink karta raha.

"Ye kya bakwaas hai"

Rohit screen ke paas face le gaya.

"Impossible."

Usne result teen baar check kiya. Refresh kiya. Laptop ko halka thappad bhi maara.

Same result.

"Ye system mere against personal war lad raha hai."

Usne instantly recheck request daali.

Few seconds later automated reply aaya.

FINAL AUTHORITY DECISION : AGENT A

Rohit freeze ho gaya.

"Agent A kaun hai"

Sameer dramatic tone me bola,

"Hidden boss."

Rohit frustration me chair par gir gaya.

"Main har subject me top hoon phir bhi fail."

Sameer chips khaate hue bola,

"Shayad unko tera face pasand nahi."

"Thanks bhai."

Lekin Rohit easily give up karne wala nahi tha.

Next year usne aur zyada preparation ki.

Subah running. Shaam simulations. Raat ko Mars navigation practice.

Uski mummy room me aayi aur dekha Rohit helmet pehenkar treadmill par bhaag raha hai.

"Beta ghar me oxygen hai. Helmet utaar."

Second attempt hua.

Phir exams. Phir top scores. Aur phir...

COGNITIVE STABILITY : FAIL

Rohit ka soul temporarily body se bahar nikal gaya.

"YE KYA HAI."

Is baar woh directly customer support AI se ladne laga.

"Explain karo."

AI calmly reply karta hai.

"Your profile does not meet Mars psychological criteria."

Rohit shocked.

"Main mentally unstable lagta hoon"

AI ne answer diya.

"You once attempted to build a homemade rocket on your terrace."

Sameer hass hass kar gir gaya.

"Bhai record me aa gaya."

Third year. Third attempt.

Ab Rohit poori city me famous ho chuka tha.

Log bolte,

"Ye wahi banda hai na jo top karke fail hota hai."

Ek uncle colony me directly bol diye,

"Beta tu Mars exam deta hai ya comedy show."

Rohit teeth grind karte hue bola,

"Ek din sabko shock dunga."

Third attempt me usne literally sab destroy kar diya.

Best physical score. Best survival score. Best navigation score.

Even instructors impressed the.

Ek instructor dusre se bola,

"This guy is one of the best candidates we have ever seen."

Meanwhile control room me Agent A quietly screen dekh rahi thi.

Ek scientist bola,

"Maam he deserves selection."

Agent A ne aankhen band ki.

"Reject him."

Scientist confused tha.

"But why are we stopping him"

Agent A ka expression suddenly emotional ho gaya.

"Because if he reaches Mars..."

Woh sentence complete nahi karti.

Screen par Rohit smile kar raha tha. Usko lag raha tha finally iss baar selection pakka hai.

Result open hua.

Aur phir wahi line.

COGNITIVE STABILITY : FAIL

Rohit ne laptop band kar diya.

Poore room me silence.

Sameer slowly bola,

"Bhai tu theek hai"

Rohit deep voice me bola,

"Nahi."

Do second silence.

Phir woh suddenly khada hua.

"Bas bohot ho gaya."

Sameer darr gaya.

"Kya karega"

Rohit ki aankhon me determination aa gaya.

"Ab investigation karunga."

Sameer blink karta raha.

"Bhai tu Mars mission ke liye apply kar raha hai ya CID join kar raha hai."

Rohit serious tone me bola,

"Jo bhi Agent A hai..."

"Ab main usse milkar rahunga."
