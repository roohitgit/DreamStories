Chapter 7: Pilot Agent M

Room me awkward silence chha gaya.

Rohit still alien revelation process kar raha tha.

Aur saamne casually khada banda usko meme ki tarah dekh raha tha.

Agent M chair par ulta baith gaya.

"So."

"Tu wahi legendary candidate hai jo har baar top karke fail hota hai."

Rohit tired face me bola,

"Haan bhai."

"Achievement unlocked."

Agent M hass pada.

"Bhai honestly Mars colony me bhi tere jokes chal rahe hain."

Rohit blink karta hai.

"Mars par bhi log mera mazaak uda rahe hain"

"Thoda sa."

Agent A annoyed tone me boli,

"M focus."

Agent M instantly straight ho gaya.

"Right."

Usne Rohit ki taraf hand extend kiya.

"Agent M."

"Pilot."

Rohit handshake karta hai.

"Rohit."

"Professional reject."

Agent M phir hass pada.

Ye banda completely opposite tha Agent A se.

Relaxed. Funny. Casual.

Jaise space travel uske liye local bus ride ho.

Agent M suddenly poochta hai,

"Waise tu seriously Mars jaana chahta hai"

"Obviously."

"Kyuki waha ka WiFi bohot slow hai."

Rohit freeze.

"KYA."

Agent M dead serious face me bola,

"Streaming impossible."

Agent A forehead pakad leti hai.

"M please useful information do."

Agent M casually hologram activate karta hai.

Aur first time Rohit proper Mars Colony Seven dekhta hai.

Massive red desert. Gigantic glass domes. Flying cargo ships. Underground transport tunnels.

Poora colony futuristic city jaisa lag raha tha.

Rohit ki aankhen literally shine karne lagi.

"Bhai."

Agent M proudly smile karta hai.

"Welcome to humanity's second home."

Rohit screen ke paas jaata hai.

"Ye sab real hai."

Agent M slowly nod karta hai.

"Earth aur Mars ke beech ab daily travel hota hai."

"Main usually shuttle pilot karta hoon."

Rohit excited ho gaya.

"Matlab tu regularly space me jaata hai."

"Haan."

"Kabhi kabhi traffic bhi milta hai orbit me."

"SPACE ME TRAFFIC."

Agent M casually shrug karta hai.

"Tourists."

Rohit ka brain again overload.

Agent A meanwhile serious thi.

"Mars situation stable nahi hai."

"Ancient structure recent weeks me zyada active hua hai."

Agent M ka smile slightly disappear ho gaya.

"Energy spikes increasing hain."

Rohit slowly poochta hai,

"Aur Sona"

Room silent.

Agent A aur Agent M ek dusre ko dekhte hain.

Agent M quietly bola,

"Complicated."

Rohit confused.

"Matlab"

Agent A hologram me Mars central dome zoom karti hai.

Huge tower visible hota hai.

"Sona Mars Colony Seven ki administrator hai."

"Brilliant scientist."

"Powerful leader."

"Problematic insaan."

Agent M whisper karta hai,

"Thoda scary bhi."

Agent A ignore karti hai.

"Usko lagta hai ancient structure humanity ko next level par le ja sakta hai."

Rohit slowly realize karta hai.

"Isliye usse meri zarurat hai."

Agent A nod karti hai.

"Tum structure activate kar sakte ho."

"Ya destroy bhi."

Rohit chair par baith gaya.

"Mujhe toh coding bhi properly nahi aati."

Agent M instantly bola,

"Aliens ne shayad low expectations rakhe honge."

Rohit hass diya.

Agent A bhi halka smile karti hai.

First time room me tension slightly kam hua.

Tabhi suddenly Agent M ka wrist communicator beep karta hai.

Incoming Mars transmission.

Screen activate hoti hai.

Aur ek woman appear hoti hai.

Elegant white outfit. Sharp eyes. Confident smile.

Sona.

Rohit instantly screen ki taraf stare karta reh gaya.

Ye wahi woman thi jo usne news channels par dekhi thi.

Sona calmly boli,

"Interesting."

"Toh finally Rohit mil hi gaya."

Agent A ka expression instantly tense ho gaya.

"Mars private channel hack karna band karo."

Sona smile karti hai.

"Tum abhi bhi overprotective ho."

Rohit confused tha.

Ye dono clearly ek dusre ko personally jaante the.

Sona directly Rohit se boli,

"Rohit."

"Mars tumhara wait kar raha hai."

Rohit nervous tone me poochta hai,

"Aur agar main nahi aaya toh"

Sona ka smile slightly dangerous ho gaya.

"Phir bhi eventually tum aaoge."

Transmission cut.

Room silent.

Agent M slowly whisper karta hai,

"Yep."

"Definitely scary."
