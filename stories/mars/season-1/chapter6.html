Chapter 6: Agent A

Rohit hidden exit tunnel se bhaagte hue finally exam center ke bahar nikla.

Uska heart full speed me dhadak raha tha.

Sameer video call par almost ro raha tha.

"Bhai tu zinda hai."

Rohit heavily breathing karte hue bola,

"Mujhe bhi abhi pata chala."

Peeche exam center me alarms abhi bhi baj rahe the. Red lights. Security drones. Flying patrol vehicles.

Aur Rohit road ke side khada bas ek hi cheez soch raha tha.

Agent A.

Woh villain nahi lag rahi thi.

Par phir woh usse Mars jaane se rok kyun rahi thi.

Sameer suspicious tone me bola,

"Bhai mujhe toh lagta hai ye sab time travel conspiracy hai."

"Tu har problem ko Marvel movie kyun bana deta hai."

Rohit ghar wapas aaya.

Lekin us raat usse neend nahi aayi.

Uske dimaag me baar baar Agent A ka sentence repeat ho raha tha.

"Mars safe nahi hai."

Subah 4 baje bhi woh room me pacing kar raha tha.

Uski mummy ne room ka door khola.

"Beta tu soya nahi"

Rohit casually bola,

"Bas Mars related life threatening conspiracy chal rahi hai."

Mummy silently door band karke chali gayi.

"Ye ladka pagal ho gaya."

Next few days Rohit continuously Mars Program updates monitor karta raha.

Aur phir ek shocking news aayi.

SPECIAL MARS REEVALUATION TEST ONLY FOR TOP REJECTED CANDIDATES

Sameer shocked.

"Bhai tujhe VIP rejection mila hai."

Rohit suspicious tha.

"Ye normal nahi hai."

Test location spaceport facility ke andar thi.

Jab Rohit waha pahucha toh atmosphere completely different tha.

No huge crowds. No public candidates.

Sirf selected few people.

Aur heavy security.

Ek officer usko andar le gaya.

Rohit whisper karta hai,

"Yahan exam kam secret mission zyada lag raha hai."

Officer ne answer nahi diya.

Woh directly ek private chamber me le jaya gaya.

Door open hua.

Andar Agent A khadi thi.

Black coat. Calm face. Sharp eyes.

Jaise hamesha.

Rohit directly bolta hai,

"Aap mujhe stalk kar rahi ho kya."

Agent A lightly smile karti hai.

"Tum already enough trouble create karte ho."

Rohit chair par baith gaya.

"Aap mujhe finally sach bataogi"

Agent A few seconds chup rahi.

Phir usne giant hologram activate kiya.

Mars Colony Seven ka map air me appear hua.

Huge domes. Underground tunnels. Research sectors.

Aur ek restricted red zone blink kar raha tha.

Rohit slowly poochta hai,

"Ye kya hai"

Agent A serious tone me boli,

"Ancient structure."

Rohit blink karta hai.

"Ancient matlab old building"

"Alien."

Poora room silent.

Rohit do second usko stare karta raha.

Phir suddenly hasa.

"Nahi seriously."

Agent A expressionless thi.

"I am serious."

Rohit ki hasi instantly gayab ho gayi.

Agent A slowly explain karti hai,

"Mars colony establish karte waqt underground ek non human structure discover hua tha."

"Technology impossible thi."

"Material unknown tha."

"Systems still partially active hain."

Rohit ka excitement aur fear dono activate ho gaye.

"Matlab aliens real hain."

Agent A calmly boli,

"Most likely."

Rohit chair se uth gaya.

"BHAI."

Agent A irritated tone me boli,

"Please normal reaction do."

"Normal reaction kaunsi hoti hai aliens sunke."

Agent A hologram zoom karti hai.

Restricted zone ke center me ek circular structure visible tha.

Aur uske around strange symbols glow kar rahe the.

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"Ye active hai"

Agent A ka expression tense ho gaya.

"Partially."

"Scientists believe kisi specific genetic pattern se ye fully activate ho sakta hai."

Rohit confused.

"Toh"

Agent A directly uski aankhon me dekhti hai.

"Tumhara DNA match karta hai."

Room silent.

Rohit ka brain officially crash ho gaya.

"MERA KYA."

Agent A slowly boli,

"Tum key ho."

Rohit chair par slowly baith gaya.

"Ek minute."

"Main bas Mars jaana chahta tha."

"Main alien remote control ka password kaise ban gaya."

Agent A first time properly hasi.

"Exactly isi liye tumhe rok rahi thi."

Rohit quietly screen ki taraf dekhta raha.

Mars suddenly ab sirf dream nahi lag raha tha.

Ab woh dangerous lag raha tha.

Tabhi room ka door suddenly open hua.

Ek young pilot andar enter hua.

Cool jacket. Relaxed smile.

Agent M.

Usne Rohit ko dekha aur grin kiya.

"Oh nice."

"Legendary failed candidate finally mil gaya."
