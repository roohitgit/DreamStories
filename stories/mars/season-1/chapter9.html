Chapter 9: Rohit Ka Decision

Mars mission approval ke baad Rohit ki life officially chaos ban chuki thi.

News channels continuously Mars mission discuss kar rahe the. Social media par memes ban rahe the. Aur Buntee har dus minute me panic call kar raha tha.

"Bhai tu seriously Mars jaa raha hai."

"Haan."

"Waha aliens hain."

"Haan."

"Waha dangerous ancient structure hai."

"Haan."

Buntee five second chup raha.

"Tu pagal hai."

Rohit hass pada.

Honestly usko bhi darr lag raha tha.

Lekin excitement bhi utni hi thi.

Bachpan se jis planet ko woh telescope se dekhta tha...

Ab woh actually uske saamne aane wala tha.

Uski mummy kitchen me silently chai bana rahi thi.

Rohit slowly paas aaya.

"Mummy."

"Tum sach me jaa rahe ho"

"Haan."

Mummy uski taraf dekhti hai.

"Tum bachpan se Mars Mars karte the."

"Mujhe laga phase hai."

Rohit halka sa smile karta hai.

"Permanent phase nikla."

Mummy emotional ho gayi.

"Lekin sambhalke rehna."

Rohit funny tone me bola,

"Aliens dikhe toh selfie bhejunga."

Mummy ne uske sir par halka tap diya.

"Joke kam karo."

Same night Agent A secretly usse milne aayi.

Rooftop. Cold wind. Night sky me Mars clearly visible tha.

Rohit telescope ke paas khada tha.

Agent A quietly uske side me aakar ruk gayi.

Few seconds dono chup rahe.

Finally Agent A boli,

"Last chance."

"Abhi bhi cancel kar sakte ho."

Rohit sky ki taraf dekhte hue bola,

"Maine bachpan se iss moment ka wait kiya hai."

"Main ab peeche nahi hatunga."

Agent A usko observe karti rahi.

"Tum samajh nahi rahe ho."

"Mars dangerous hai."

"Structure unpredictable hai."

"Sona obsessed ho chuki hai."

Rohit quietly poochta hai,

"Aap itna care kyun karti ho"

Agent A instantly silent ho gayi.

Uski eyes me complicated emotions the.

Jaise woh answer dena chahti ho... par de nahi sakti.

Finally woh slowly boli,

"Kyuki maine already bohot kuch lose kiya hai."

Rohit confused tha.

Agent A immediately topic change karti hai.

"Agent M tumhe Mars le jayega."

Rohit smile karta hai.

"Space Uber."

Agent A almost smile kar deti hai.

"Please serious raho ek minute."

Rohit telescope se Mars dekhte hue softly bola,

"Mujhe darr lag raha hai."

Agent A shocked expression me uski taraf dekhti hai.

"Really"

"Haan."

"Par excitement zyada hai."

Agent A slowly nod karti hai.

"Typical human."

Rohit grin karta hai.

"Aapne khud bola tha humans stubborn hote hain."

Few seconds silence.

Cold wind rooftop par flow kar rahi thi.

Phir Agent A finally ek shocking line bolti hai.

"Fine."

Rohit confused.

"Fine matlab"

"Main bhi mission par chalungi."

Rohit literally choke kar gaya.

"KYA."

"Aap toh mujhe rok rahi thi."

"Ab protect karungi."

Rohit shocked expression me stare karta raha.

"Ye sudden character development kaise hua."

Agent A eyes roll karti hai.

"Tumhe akela Mars bhejna dangerous hai."

Rohit smile karta hai.

"Honestly mujhe bhi lagta hai."

Next day official briefing start hui.

Huge spaceport facility me media aur scientists bhare hue the.

Massive spacecraft launch pad par ready khada tha.

Aur first time Rohit ne uska naam dekha.

AURORA 1

Silver body. Blue engine glow. Gigantic boosters.

Poora ship unreal lag raha tha.

Agent M already pilot uniform me khada tha.

Usne Rohit ko dekhkar whistle maari.

"Finally."

"Mars ka chosen wifi password aa gaya."

Rohit hass diya.

Agent A side se boli,

"M please."

Suddenly media crowd me noise hua.

Sona giant holographic screen par appear hui.

Poora spaceport silent ho gaya.

Sona calmly boli,

"Humanity ek naye era me enter karne wali hai."

"Rohit."

"Mars tumhara wait kar raha hai."

Transmission end.

Rohit ka heart fast beat karne laga.

Ab ye dream officially real ban chuka tha.

Aur somewhere deep below Mars surface...

Ancient alien structure slowly glow karne laga.
