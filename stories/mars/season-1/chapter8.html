Chapter 8: Mars Authority

Earth par officially sab kuch normal lag raha tha.

People office ja rahe the. Traffic jam chal raha tha. Food delivery drones sky me ud rahe the.

Lekin Rohit ki life completely upside down ho chuki thi.

Aliens real the. Mars par ancient structure tha. Aur somehow woh uska key tha.

Normal insaan iss point par therapy leta.

Rohit YouTube par Mars documentaries dekh raha tha.

Buntee room me enter hua.

"Bhai tu stress me bhi documentaries dekh raha hai"

"Research."

"Ye obsession hai."

Rohit laptop rotate karta hai.

Screen par Mars Colony Seven ka live broadcast chal raha tha.

Gigantic glass domes red desert ke beech shine kar rahe the. Hover vehicles hawa me move kar rahe the. Aur center tower ke top par Mars flag wave kar raha tha.

Buntee impressed ho gaya.

"Okay ye honestly bohot cool lag raha hai."

Rohit quietly screen dekh raha tha.

Phir suddenly broadcast par Sona appear hui.

Elegant white suit. Calm expression. Sharp eyes.

Poora colony usko respect se dekh raha tha.

Reporter poochta hai,

"Colony expansion project ka future kya hai"

Sona confidently boli,

"Mars humanity ka next step hai."

"Earth sirf beginning thi."

People clap karne lage.

Buntee whisper karta hai,

"Bhai ye toh proper sci fi queen lag rahi hai."

Rohit slowly nod karta hai.

"Thodi dangerous queen."

Broadcast suddenly switch hua.

Mars underground research footage appear hua.

Scientists strange glowing structure examine kar rahe the.

Alien symbols walls par shine kar rahe the.

Aur ek second ke liye camera glitch hua.

Rohit freeze ho gaya.

Usne clearly structure ke center me ek circular slot dekha.

Exactly human hand size.

Buntee nervous tone me bola,

"Bhai mujhe tera face yaad aa gaya."

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"Ye mere liye bana hai."

Meanwhile Mars Colony Seven me Sona private observation deck par khadi thi.

Huge glass window ke bahar red storms visible the.

Aur neeche poori colony glowing lights me shine kar rahi thi.

Ek assistant aaya.

"Maam."

"Rohit abhi bhi Earth par hai."

Sona calm tone me boli,

"Temporary."

Assistant nervous lag raha tha.

"Agent A usko still protect kar rahi hai."

Sona slowly smile karti hai.

"Meri sister hamesha emotional decisions leti hai."

Assistant shocked expression me uski taraf dekhta hai.

"Toh aap sach me usse force karengi"

Sona window ke bahar Mars desert dekhte hue whisper karti hai,

"Humanity future se darr nahi sakti."

"Structure activate hona hi chahiye."

Earth par same time Agent A secretly Rohit se milne aayi.

Night time. Rainy weather. Parking area completely empty.

Rohit hoodie pehenkar wait kar raha tha.

Agent A black vehicle se bahar aayi.

Uska expression tense tha.

"Situation worse ho rahi hai."

Rohit instantly poochta hai,

"Sona kya kar rahi hai"

Agent A directly answer nahi deti.

Woh ek holographic file open karti hai.

Aur Rohit ka DNA scan screen par appear hota hai.

Alien symbols uske genetic pattern ke around glow kar rahe the.

Rohit confused.

"Mere genes me WiFi router laga hai kya."

Agent A ignore karti hai.

"Ancient structure tumhe detect kar raha hai."

"Distance matter nahi karta."

Rohit ka smile slowly disappear ho gaya.

"Kya matlab"

Agent A serious tone me boli,

"Every day structure zyada active ho raha hai."

"Mars systems random signals detect kar rahe hain."

"Energy spikes increasing hain."

"Scientists panic me hain."

Rohit quietly poochta hai,

"Aur Sona"

Agent A ka expression complicated ho gaya.

"Usko lagta hai structure humanity ko galaxy level civilization bana sakta hai."

"Par mujhe lagta hai..."

Woh sentence complete nahi karti.

Rohit slowly bolta hai,

"Ki kuch galat ho sakta hai."

Agent A nod karti hai.

Few seconds silence.

Rain ki awaaz parking area me echo ho rahi thi.

Rohit finally direct question poochta hai,

"Aap mujhe Mars jaane se rokogi"

Agent A uski taraf dekhti hai.

Uski eyes me genuine fear tha.

"Haan."

Rohit slowly smile karta hai.

"Late ho gaya."

Agent A confused.

Rohit pocket se notification screen dikhata hai.

OFFICIAL MARS MISSION APPROVAL STATUS : ACCEPTED

Agent A freeze ho gayi.

"Tumhe kisne approve kiya"

Rohit slowly answer deta hai,

"Sona."

Poora atmosphere instantly heavy ho gaya.

Aur first time Agent A genuinely scared lag rahi thi.
