Chapter 10: Mars Mission

Launch day finally aa gaya.

Poora Earth mission dekh raha tha.

News channels nonstop live coverage kar rahe the. Social media par hashtags trend ho rahe the. Aur Buntee already emotional breakdown mode me tha.

"Bhai tu sach me space me jaa raha hai."

"Haan."

"Wapas aa jaana."

"Try karunga."

"TRY KARUNGA matlab kya hota hai."

Spaceport facility thousands lights se glow kar rahi thi.

Massive launch tower sky ko touch karta hua lag raha tha.

Aur center me khadi thi humanity ki most advanced spacecraft.

AURORA 1

Silver body morning sunlight me shine kar rahi thi. Blue engines softly glow kar rahe the. Aur giant Mars emblem side par visible tha.

Rohit bas ship ko dekhte reh gaya.

"Bhai."

"Ye real hai."

Agent M pilot uniform me aaya.

Cool sunglasses. Confident smile. Zero tension.

"Welcome to space airlines."

Rohit grin karta hai.

"Luggage limit kya hai."

"Bas aliens allowed nahi hain."

Agent A side se aa gayi.

Black mission suit me woh aur bhi intimidating lag rahi thi.

"M focus."

Agent M salute karta hai.

"Yes boss."

Mission crew final preparation kar raha tha. Scientists systems check kar rahe the. Robots cargo load kar rahe the. Ground staff launch coordinates verify kar raha tha.

Aur media drones har angle se recording kar rahe the.

Rohit suddenly nervous feel karne laga.

Usne slowly poochta hai,

"Space sickness hota hai kya"

Agent M casually bola,

"Sometimes."

"Vomiting bhi."

Rohit ka face instantly serious ho gaya.

"Main Earth par hi theek hoon."

Agent A almost hasi rok nahi paayi.

Mission countdown start hua.

T minus 60 minutes.

Crew launch preparation room me shift hua.

Waha futuristic suits ready the.

Rohit apna Mars suit dekhkar excited ho gaya.

Red and black armored design. Helmet me holographic HUD. Back par oxygen system.

"Ye toh Iron Man suit lag raha hai."

Agent M proudly bola,

"Bas ud nahi sakta."

"Disappointing."

Suit pehente waqt Rohit almost gir gaya.

"Ye itna heavy kyun hai."

Agent M bola,

"Space me survive karne ke liye."

"Fashion ke liye nahi."

Finally crew launch elevator me enter hua.

Huge metallic elevator slowly rocket ke top ki taraf move karne laga.

Neeche poora launch complex visible tha.

Rohit quietly Earth horizon dekhta raha.

Ye wahi planet tha jahan usne poori life spend ki thi.

Aur ab woh usse leave karne wala tha.

Agent A softly poochti hai,

"Nervous"

"Thoda."

Agent M instantly bola,

"Good."

"Matlab tum normal ho."

Launch chamber open hua.

Aur finally crew Aurora 1 ke andar enter hua.

Interior unbelievable tha.

Blue ambient lights. Floating hologram controls. Huge digital windows. Advanced AI systems.

Rohit seat par baithte hue whisper karta hai,

"Main sapna dekh raha hoon kya."

AI voice suddenly activate hui.

WELCOME ABOARD AURORA 1

Rohit literally jump kar gaya.

"Bhai ship bolti bhi hai."

Agent M cockpit me baith gaya.

"Sabse zyada attitude bhi isi ka hai."

Countdown continue hua.

T minus 10 minutes.

Suddenly main screen activate hui.

Sona live transmission par appear hui.

White royal outfit. Calm expression. Sharp glowing eyes.

Poora spacecraft silent ho gaya.

Sona directly Rohit ko dekhkar boli,

"Finally."

"Mars ka key aa raha hai."

Rohit awkward smile deta hai.

"Pressure mat do."

Sona halka sa smile karti hai.

"Mars tumhe change kar dega."

Transmission cut.

Agent A ka expression instantly dark ho gaya.

Rohit quietly poochta hai,

"Aap usse trust nahi karti"

Agent A softly whisper karti hai,

"Main usse bohot achhe se jaanti hoon."

Countdown final stage me enter hua.

T minus 30 seconds.

Aurora 1 engines ignite hue.

Poora ship violently vibrate karne laga.

Outside giant flames launch pad ke around explode hui.

Rohit seat tightly pakad leta hai.

"BHAI."

Agent M grin karta hai.

"First launch always crazy hota hai."

AI voice echo hui.

TEN NINE EIGHT

Rohit heart racing feel kar raha tha.

SEVEN SIX FIVE

Earth ka future iss mission par depend kar raha tha.

FOUR THREE TWO ONE

LAUNCH

Aurora 1 sky ki taraf blast kar gayi.

Massive acceleration ne sabko seats me push kar diya.

Clouds instantly neeche reh gaye.

Blue sky black space me convert hone laga.

Aur first time...

Rohit ne Earth ko space se dekha.

Beautiful blue planet silently darkness me shine kar raha tha.

Rohit slowly whisper karta hai,

"Wow."

Agent M smile karta hai.

"Welcome to space."

Meanwhile millions kilometers away... deep below Mars surface...

Ancient alien structure fully awaken hone laga.

Red symbols walls par glow kar rahe the. Energy waves tunnels me spread ho rahi thi.

Aur center chamber me ek ancient voice echo hui.

"THE KEY APPROACHES."

Season 1 End
