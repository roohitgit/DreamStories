Chapter 4: Rohit Ka Gussa

Teen baar fail hone ke baad Rohit officially frustrated insaan ban chuka tha.

Uske room ki condition bhi uske mood jaisi thi.

Mars posters. Open laptops. Energy drink cans. Aur ek whiteboard jisme giant letters me likha tha:

WHO IS AGENT A

Buntee room me enter hua aur board dekhkar ruk gaya.

"Bhai tu Mars jaana chahta hai ya Netflix crime documentary banana"

Rohit serious face me screen dekh raha tha.

"Something is wrong."

Usne multiple candidate records hack type style me open kar rakhe the.

"Har saal mera score top."

"Har saal same rejection."

Buntee chair par baith gaya.

"Shayad Agent A ko tera hairstyle pasand nahi."

"Main serious hoon."

Rohit ne ek file open ki.

"Look at this."

Screen par multiple rejected candidates ke names the.

Lekin unme ek common thing thi.

Sab normal scores wale candidates the. Sirf Rohit exceptional tha.

Aur phir bhi reject.

Buntee ka expression bhi serious ho gaya.

"Matlab specifically tujhe hi rok rahe hain."

Rohit slowly khada hua.

"Exactly."

Usne coffee cup uthaya. Ek sip li. Aur instantly thook diya.

"Cold ho gayi."

Buntee bola,

"Investigation me coffee ki jaan ja rahi hai."

Rohit ne Mars Program database access karna start kiya.

Firewall alerts. Security warnings. AI verification.

Sab kuch futuristic tha.

Lekin Rohit bhi stubborn level infinity tha.

Finally ek hidden administrative log open hua.

Aur usme repeatedly ek naam flash ho raha tha.

AGENT A

Buntee dramatic tone me bola,

"Villain reveal."

Rohit screen zoom karta hai.

"Every rejection approved by Agent A."

Usne aur deeper records search kiye.

Mars transfers. Confidential operations. Special missions.

Sab jagah Agent A ka involvement tha.

Ek image bhi thi.

Black coat. Sharp eyes. Calm expression.

Buntee slowly bola,

"Bhai ye toh dangerous lag rahi hai."

Rohit stare karta raha.

"Ye mujhe Mars kyun nahi jaane dena chahti."

Tabhi suddenly system alarm activate ho gaya.

ACCESS VIOLATION DETECTED

Room me panic.

Buntee jump kar gaya.

"Bhai police aa jayegi."

Rohit fast typing karne laga.

"Relax."

"Main bas dekh raha tha."

"Tu Mars database hack kar raha hai aur bol raha hai bas dekh raha tha."

Screen suddenly black ho gayi.

Aur ek single message appear hua.

STOP SEARCHING

Room silent.

Buntee slowly peeche hatne laga.

"Nahi bhai. Main iss horror movie ka part nahi banunga."

Rohit ki aankhen narrow ho gayi.

"Someone is watching."

Usi waqt Mars orbit station me ek woman quietly monitor screen dekh rahi thi.

Agent A.

Uske side me ek young pilot khada tha.

Cool jacket. Relaxed attitude. Confident smile.

Agent M.

Agent M casually bola,

"Rohit fir se database me ghus gaya."

Agent A ne sigh li.

"Expected."

Agent M screen par Rohit ko dekhkar hasa.

"Honestly mujhe ye banda pasand aa raha hai."

Agent A serious tone me boli,

"Ye joke nahi hai."

Agent M slowly poochta hai,

"Aap usse kab tak rokengi"

Agent A chup ho gayi.

Few seconds silence.

Phir woh whisper karti hai,

"Jab tak possible hai."

Meanwhile Earth par Rohit investigation continue kar raha tha.

Usne Mars Program ke upcoming exam locations search kiye.

Aur ek line dekhte hi uski aankhen chamak uthi.

SPECIAL EXAMINATION EVENT DIRECT OVERSIGHT : AGENT A

Location nearby city me thi.

Buntee ne screen dekhi.

"Bhai please bata tu kya soch raha hai."

Rohit slowly smile karta hai.

"Simple."

"Ab result ka wait nahi karunga."

Buntee nervous ho gaya.

"Matlab"

Rohit dramatic tone me bola,

"Main directly Agent A se milunga."

Buntee ka face white pad gaya.

"Bhai tu Mars exam dene gaya tha."

"Ab spy thriller start ho gaya."
