Chapter 2: Mars Selection Exams

Mars Human Settlement Program duniya ka toughest recruitment process mana jata tha.

Sirf intelligent hona enough nahi tha.

Physical strength. Mental stability. Zero gravity control. Survival skills. Sab kuch test hota tha.

Aur iss saal lakho candidates apply kar chuke the.

Rohit subah 5 baje hi exam center pahunch gaya.

Huge futuristic building dekhkar uski aankhen chamak uthi.

Glass walls. Hover drones. Robot security. Aur giant Mars hologram entrance ke upar rotate kar raha tha.

Rohit dheere se bola,

"Bhai ye exam center kam aur Avengers headquarters zyada lag raha hai."

Uska dost Sameer video call par tha.

"Tu pakka exam dene gaya hai na selfie lene nahi"

Security robot Rohit ke saamne aaya.

"Please stand still for scanning."

Robot ne scan complete kiya.

"Stress level detected."

Rohit bola,

"Mars jaana hai bhai Goa trip nahi."

Inside hall thousands candidates baithe hue the.

Koi formulas revise kar raha tha. Koi meditation. Koi protein shake pee raha tha.

Aur ek banda corner me quietly ro raha tha.

Rohit uske paas gaya.

"Bhai sab theek"

Candidate bola,

"Mummy ne bola tha government job try kar."

Announcement speakers suddenly activate hue.

"All candidates report for physical endurance test."

Sab stadium ki taraf bhaagne lage.

Ek strict instructor black suit me khada tha.

Uska face itna serious tha jaise woh smile karne par tax bhar deta ho.

"First test. Ten kilometer endurance run."

Poore crowd se pain wali awaaz nikli.

Race start hui.

Candidates full speed me bhaagne lage.

Ek banda Rohit ke side me bhaag raha tha.

"Bhai Mars par bhi itna bhaagna padega kya"

Rohit bola,

"Gravity kam hogi. Shayad flying run hoga."

Candidate confused ho gaya.

Half race ke baad ek candidate dramatic style me ground par gir gaya.

"Main nahi kar sakta."

Instructor chillaya,

"Mars tumhare emotions ka wait nahi karega."

Rohit side se bhaagte hue bola,

"Motivational speaker dangerous hai."

Final lap me Rohit top group me tha.

Finish line cross karte hi woh ground par baith gaya.

"Earth ki gravity personal dushmani rakhti hai."

Agla round aur zyada dangerous tha.

Zero gravity chamber.

Huge circular room me artificial gravity band kar di gayi.

Aur chaos instantly start ho gaya.

Ek banda hawa me ulta spin hone laga. Dusra directly wall se takra gaya. Teesra panic me hawa me swimming kar raha tha.

"Mujhe neeche utaaro."

Rohit surprisingly stable tha.

Woh calmly float kar raha tha.

Instructor impressed ho gaya.

"Good control."

Rohit proudly bola,

"Sir bachpan se terrace par practice ki hai."

Instructor silently chala gaya.

Ek candidate whisper karta hai,

"Ye banda insaan kam satellite zyada lag raha hai."

Next test Mars survival simulation tha.

Candidates ko virtual Mars storm me survive karna tha.

Low oxygen. Dust storm. System failures.

Most candidates panic kar gaye.

Lekin Rohit weirdly calm tha.

Usne oxygen conserve ki. Emergency shelter activate kiya. Aur damaged system bhi repair kar diya.

Simulation end hote hi AI voice aayi.

"Candidate performance excellent."

Rohit chest out karke bola,

"Mars ready ho ja."

Meanwhile control room me koi quietly usko observe kar raha tha.

Black coat. Sharp eyes. Calm face.

Agent A.

Ek scientist excited tone me bola,

"Maam candidate Rohit exceptional hai."

Agent A screen se nazar hataye bina boli,

"I know."

Scientist slowly poochta hai,

"Toh iss baar select karna hai"

Agent A ka expression suddenly serious ho gaya.

"No."

Scientist shock me reh gaya.

"But why"

Agent A quietly whisper karti hai,

"Because Mars is waiting for him."
