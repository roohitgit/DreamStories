ABOUT HIT STORIES

Real dreams transformed into story universes.

OUR VISION

HIT STORIES is built from dreams that I saw in real life. Sometimes those dreams felt incomplete, mysterious, cinematic, or too interesting to forget. So I started writing them as stories.

Some parts are exactly how I saw them in my dreams, while some parts are expanded with imagination, extra twists, mysteries, and fun moments to complete the journey.

Every story here is a mix of real dreams, fiction, sci-fi imagination, and emotional storytelling. The goal is simple: to make readers feel like they are inside a dream that slowly unfolds into a complete universe.



WHAT YOU WILL FIND

• Stories inspired by real dreams

• sci-fi adventures

• Mystery and suspense elements

• Multi-chapter story universes

• Original characters and worlds

• Stories expanded beyond the dream itself



THE JOURNEY AHEAD

HIT STORIES will continue growing with more dream-inspired stories, new universes, new mysteries, and unexpected adventures.

Some stories may continue for multiple seasons, while some dreams may evolve into completely different worlds.

This is only the beginning of turning dreams into story experiences.
